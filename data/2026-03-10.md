### [1] [RISCBench: Benchmarking RISC-V Orchestration Efficiency in FPGA and FPGA-Like Computing Engines](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06580)
*Dave Ojika,Projjal Gupta,Preethi Budi,Herman Lam,Shreya Mehrotra*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: RISCBench：一个用于量化异构系统中RISC-V编排引擎效率的基准测试套件和方法论，引入SIT（持续瞬时吞吐量）指标来评估超越峰值性能的持续效率


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 异构系统越来越多地使用RISC-V核心作为编排引擎来管理加速器和可重构结构之间的数据移动、同步和调度。传统的性能指标（如FLOPs、TOPS/W或每操作能耗）无法捕捉编排效率，而编排效率往往决定了系统的持续行为。随着系统向紧密耦合的异构结构和共封装加速器发展，控制平面行为决定了这些平台是否能实现其承诺的性能。

Method: 提出了RISCBench，一个内核基准测试套件和开放方法论，用于量化编排效率。引入了持续瞬时吞吐量（SIT）指标，该指标在接近聚合的执行间隔内累积瞬时吞吐量，捕捉超越峰值速率的持续效率。该方法在代表性平台上进行评估，涵盖软核和硬核RISC-V编排引擎，包括基于FPGA的原型设计和加速器级实现。

Result: 结果突显了同步和数据驻留驱动的权衡，这些权衡限制了超越峰值性能的实际吞吐量，表明SIT作为评估异构系统和AI推理应用中编排效率的实用、平台无关描述符的价值。

Conclusion: RISCBench和SIT指标为量化异构系统中RISC-V编排引擎的效率提供了有效的方法，填补了传统性能指标无法评估控制平面效率的空白，对于评估现代异构计算平台的实际性能具有重要意义。

Abstract: Heterogeneous systems increasingly rely on RISC-V cores as orchestration engines to manage data movement, synchronization, and scheduling across accelerators and reconfigurable fabrics. Conventional performance metrics, such as FLOPs, TOPS/W, or energy per operation, do not capture orchestration efficiency, even though it often dictates sustained system behavior. This gap is increasingly relevant as systems evolve toward tightly coupled heterogeneous fabrics and co-packaged accelerators, where control-plane behavior determines whether these platforms achieve their promised performance.
  We present RISCBench, a kernel benchmark suite and open methodology for quantifying orchestration efficiency. RISCBench introduces the Sustained Instantaneous Throughput (SIT) metric, which accumulates instantaneous throughput over near-aggregate execution intervals, capturing sustained efficiency beyond peak rates. The methodology is evaluated across representative platforms spanning soft and hard RISC-V orchestration engines, including FPGA-based prototyping and accelerator-class implementations. Results highlight synchronization and data residency driven tradeoffs that limit realized throughput beyond peak performance, motivating SIT as a practical, platform-independent descriptor for evaluating orchestration efficiency in heterogeneous systems and AI inference applications.

### [2] [Converting Binary Floating-Point Numbers to Shortest Decimal Strings: An Experimental Review](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06581)
*Jaël Champagne Gareau,Daniel Lemire*

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在共享或记录数值数据时，需要将二进制浮点数转换为十进制字符串表示。虽然工程师已经开发了许多准确、简短的字符串生成算法，但需要了解不同算法在实际硬件架构和数据集上的性能表现，以及现有实现的质量问题。

Method: 通过实证比较方法，在不同硬件架构和数据集上测试各种浮点数转十进制字符串算法。分析了包括Schubfach、Dragonbox、Dragon4等算法，并考察了C++、Swift等语言标准库的实现。

Result: 最新算法如Schubfach和Dragonbox比Steele和White的Dragon4快10倍，仅需210条指令，而Dragon4需要1500-5000条指令。但所有被调查的实现都不能始终生成最短字符串，有些输出比最优长度长30%。标准库实现执行指令数显著多于最快方法，性能差距因CPU架构和编译器而异。

Conclusion: 浮点数转十进制字符串算法在性能上有显著提升空间，最新算法比传统方法快10倍。但现有实现普遍存在输出字符串长度非最优的问题。标准库实现性能落后，需要优化。论文为未来研究提出了优化目标。

Abstract: When sharing or logging numerical data, we must convert binary floating-point numbers into their decimal string representations. For example, the number $π$ might become 3.1415927. Engineers have perfected many algorithms for producing such accurate, short strings. We present an empirical comparison across diverse hardware architectures and datasets. Cutting-edge techniques like Schubfach and Dragonbox achieve up to a tenfold speedup over Steele and White's Dragon4, executing as few as 210 instructions per conversion compared to Dragon4's 1500-5000 instructions. Often per their specification, none of the implementations we surveyed consistently produced the shortest possible strings-some generate outputs up to 30% longer than optimal. We find that standard library implementations in languages such as C++ and Swift execute significantly more instructions than the fastest methods, with performance gaps varying across CPU architectures and compilers. We suggest some optimization targets for future research.
