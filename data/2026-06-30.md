### [1] [Evidence-Driven LLM Agent for C-to-Synthesizable-C Conversion and Verification](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.28409)
*Zhe Zhao,Hongbing Lang,Zhihan Xiao,Luke Ztz Hu,John Imoleayo Adebisi,Songping Mai*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一个面向C到HLS-C转换的闭环生成-验证-诊断-修复工作流，包含合作代理、渐进式失配定位链和基于检索增强生成的修复机制，显著优于现有方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有HLS工具链对C代码有严格的语法子集限制，导致大量软件可编译的C程序无法完成HLS流程。而现有的LLM系统仅覆盖早期流水线阶段，且直接使用原始工具日志，导致修复效果脆弱且难以复现。

Method: 提出了三个核心组件：(1) 基于合作代理的端到端闭环工作流，由四阶段验证器严格隔离证据；(2) 渐进式失配定位链(PMLC)，通过日志归一化、AST反向切片和双迹线插桩定位CSim/CoSim失配；(3) 带类型查询的两阶段证据RAG，基于自演化、按家族路由的修复卡片池进行检索增强修复。

Result: 实验结果表明，该工作流在所有可比的最先进模型中显著优于现有方法。

Conclusion: 该闭环工作流通过多代理协作、精细化失配定位和检索增强修复，有效解决了C到HLS-C转换中的四阶段验证难题，为HLS代码修复提供了更可靠、可复现的解决方案。

Abstract: Software-compilable C programs routinely fail to complete the four-stage pipeline of a high-level synthesis (HLS) toolchain -- compilation, C simulation (CSim), synthesis, and C/RTL co-simulation (CoSim) -- because HLS accepts only a synthesizable subset of C (HLS-C). Yet most existing large language model (LLM) systems built for HLS code repair only cover the early pipeline stages and feed raw tool logs directly to the model, yielding brittle and hard-to-reproduce fixes. We formulate C-to-HLS-C conversion as a closed-loop generation-verification-diagnosis-repair problem on an HLS tool (Xilinx Vitis), contributing three components: an end-to-end workflow of cooperating agents closed by the four-stage verifier under strict evidence isolation; a Progressive Mismatch Localization Chain (PMLC) that localizes CSim/CoSim mismatches through log normalization, AST backward slicing, and dual-trace instrumentation; and a typed-query, two-stage evidence RAG backed by a self-evolving, family-routed repair-card pool. Experimental results show that the proposed workflow substantially outperforms all comparable state-of-the-art models.

### [2] [SHIFT: Dynamic Compute Relocation Framework for Communication-Aware Chiplet-Based Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.28754)
*Arvin Delavari,Leonid Popryho,Inna Partin-Vaisband,Boris Vaisband*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: SHIFT是一种拓扑无关的芯片架构方法，通过转移计算节点上下文和数据而非仅移动数据来优化异构系统中的通信，在芯片级架构上结合改进的最短路径算法和轻量级ML辅助策略，在随机测试中实现75.2%-97.9%的成功迁移率、16.4%-62.5%的平均延迟改善和最高12.5倍的吞吐量提升，并在LLM工作负载上实现4.9倍运行时间、5.9倍吞吐量和1.8倍能效的改进。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 大规模异构系统的通信复杂度日益增加，传统片上网络仅在节点间转移数据，缺乏对计算节点上下文迁移的考虑，限制了通信感知的负载放置和路由优化的效率。

Method: 提出SHIFT框架，在基于chiplet的细间距集成平台上，利用多层路由在功能/内存chiplet和作为智能节点的utility chiplet之间，通过改进的最短路径算法和轻量级ML辅助策略实现自适应调度与路由，实现计算节点上下文和数据的整体迁移。

Result: 随机测试中：迁移成功率75.2%-97.9%，平均延迟改善16.4%-62.5%（最大76.8%），吞吐量提升最高12.5倍，单位面积功耗降低约8%，能效提升最高58.3%，性能提升18%。LLM工作负载上：运行时间提升4.9倍，吞吐量提升5.9倍，能效提升1.8倍。

Conclusion: SHIFT在芯片级异构系统上有效降低通信开销，显著提升延迟、吞吐量和能效，超越现有晶圆级LLM服务，具备大规模平台兼容性。

Abstract: The increasing communication complexity of large-scale heterogeneous systems has motivated runtime methodologies for communication-aware workload placement and routing optimization. These communication limitations are addressed in this paper by proposing SHIFT, a novel topology-agnostic approach that transfers compute node context and data to a more suitably positioned node, rather than only shifting data as in conventional networks-on-chip. The proposed strategy is evaluated on a chiplet-based architecture utilizing a fine-pitch integration platform featuring multiple bandwidth-domains for heterogeneous workloads. The proposed architecture employs multi-layered routing between functional or memory chiplets and utility chiplets, which serve as intelligent nodes for routing and compute relocation. Adaptive scheduling and routing utilize a modified shortest-path algorithm for large-scale systems, complemented by a lightweight ML-assisted policy that infers traffic conditions to improve adaptivity. To establish a performance baseline, the initial assessment uses random instruction vectors and data patterns to evaluate the fundamental capabilities of SHIFT. Simulation results exhibit successful relocations over total trials ranging from 75.2% to 97.9% across configurations, with average latency improvements of 16.4%-62.5% and a maximum of 76.8%. In addition, throughput is improved by up to 12.5x, power dissipation per unit area is reduced by ~8%, energy-per-bit is reduced by up to 58.3%, and performance is improved by 18%. To evaluate efficiency under high logic and data density, the framework was tested on standard LLM workloads. Results exhibit average improvements of 4.9x, 5.9x, and 1.8x in runtime, throughput, and energy-efficiency, respectively, surpassing state-of-the-art wafer-scale LLM services and demonstrating compatibility with large-scale platforms and applications.

### [3] [Harvesting AI Computation at the Edge via Generic Approximation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29518)

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 提出了一种利用空闲AI计算资源处理通用任务的框架，通过NAS将传统任务转换为神经网络模型，在AI芯片空闲时调度执行，提升边缘处理器整体性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 1) 利用神经架构搜索(NAS)将通用计算任务自动转换为神经网络模型；2) 设计运行时调度器，在不影响主要AI工作负载的前提下，将近似任务卸载到空闲的AI芯片上执行。

Abstract: With the widespread adoption of AI in various IoT scenarios such as smart sensing and processing, AI chips have become a common component at the edge. These chips are typically specialized for structured neural network (NN) processing and are designed to meet peak workload demands. Conversely, general-purpose processing engines at the edge may struggle with compute-intensive tasks such as signal processing and complex numerical operations because of stringent resource constraints. To address this imbalance, we propose a framework that harvests unused AI computation resources using general-purpose approximation techniques. The core idea is to automatically convert traditional computing tasks into neural network models via a representative neural architecture search (NAS) method. These approximate versions of general-purpose tasks are then deployed on AI engines during their idle periods. Specifically, we introduce a runtime scheduler that offloads these tasks to AI chips without compromising the performance of primary AI workloads, thereby alleviating the burden on general-purpose processors. Experiments on a representative AIoT processor show that our proposed AI computation harvesting strategy delivers substantial performance improvements across a set of edge processing tasks.

### [4] [HBM Is Not All You Need: Efficient Disaggregated LLM Serving across Memory-heterogeneous Accelerators](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29986)
*Zhixiang Wei,Yun Wang,James Yen,Mingyuan Xia,Zhengwei Qi*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: HMA-Serve提出了一种面向异构内存加速器的解耦式LLM推理系统，将基于GDDR的加速器用于Prefill阶段，基于HBM的GPU用于Decode阶段，通过逐阶段量化、计算-传输流水线和延迟反量化三项技术，在跨厂商环境下实现了最高3.2倍的有效吞吐量提升和4.8倍的单位成本有效吞吐量提升。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有LLM推理将Prefill（计算密集型）和Decode（内存密集型）解耦到不同硬件上，但数据中心GPU的HBM带宽在Prefill阶段几乎完全闲置，造成资源浪费。若采用GDDR加速器进行Prefill、HBM GPU进行Decode的异构方案，可大幅降低成本，但会面临跨厂商导致的KV格式不兼容和软件栈缺失两大问题。

Method: (1) 逐阶段量化：Prefill采用厂商原生的低精度计算以追求高吞吐，Decode保持BF16高精度； (2) 计算-传输流水线：将每层KV缓存的传输与后续层的Prefill计算重叠，降低首Token延迟(TTFT)； (3) 延迟反量化：传输原始量化字节，在Decode GPU上按需重建，减少网络带宽和HBM占用。

Result: 在Qwen3模型（4B-32B）和三个生产轨迹上，HMA-Serve相比最先进的同构内存方法实现最高3.2倍的有效吞吐量提升，4.8倍的单位成本有效吞吐量提升，且生成质量基准测试无可测量的损失。

Conclusion: HMA-Serve验证了跨厂商异构内存加速器用于LLM解耦推理的可行性，通过三项关键技术有效解决了KV格式不兼容和软件栈缺失问题，实现了显著的成本效益提升，为未来数据中心LLM推理的硬件选型提供了新思路。

Abstract: LLM inference comprises a compute-bound prefill phase and a memory-bound decode phase, and recent systems disaggregate them onto separate hardware. Yet today's datacenter GPUs rely on costly HBM whose bandwidth sits almost entirely idle during prefill. LLM serving across memory-heterogeneous accelerators (MemHA) pairs GDDR-based accelerators for prefill with HBM-based GPUs for decode, promising lower cost without sacrificing performance. Pushed to its most economical form, MemHA serving is inherently cross-vendor, since the best-suited chip for each phase may come from a different vendor. This breaks two assumptions that single-vendor disaggregation takes for granted -- a KV format both ends consume natively, and a shared software stack. We present \textbf{HMA-Serve}, a MemHA-centric disaggregated serving system pairing GDDR-based accelerators for prefill with HBM-based GPUs for decode efficiently. HMA-Serve achieves this through (1) phase-wise quantization, applying vendor-native low precision for high-throughput prefill while keeping decode in high-precision BF16, (2) a compute-transfer pipeline that overlaps each layer's KV cache transfer with later-layer prefill to reduce time-to-first-token (TTFT), and (3) deferred dequantization, shipping raw quantized bytes and reconstructing them lazily on the decode GPU to reduce network bandwidth and HBM usage. Across four Qwen3 models (4B--32B) and three production traces, HMA-Serve delivers up to $3.2\times$ higher goodput than state-of-the-art memory-homogeneous methods and $4.8\times$ higher goodput-per-dollar, with no measurable loss on generation-quality benchmarks.

</details>
