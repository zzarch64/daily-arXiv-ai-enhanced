### [1] [Constant Depth Threshold Circuits For Exhaustive Epistasis Detection](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29719)
*André Ribeiro,Aleksandar Ilic,Leonel Sousa*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出基于阈门电路（threshold gate）的专用神经形态硬件设计，用于解决生物信息学中计算复杂度极高的上位性检测（Epistasis Detection）问题。通过数据编码、LIF神经元组合生成策略及恒定深度的阈门种群计数电路，实现了无额外复杂度开销的运行时间，仅受组合数限制，且空间复杂度为对数线性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 大规模神经形态硬件的发展使得阈门电路的实际应用成为可能，而阈门电路相较于传统计算类具有复杂度优势。作者希望利用这一优势，解决生物信息学中计算最复杂的组合问题之一——上位性检测，以突破传统方法在高计算复杂度下的瓶颈。

Method: 1) 提出专用流水线电路，利用共置内存和可配置并行性高效计算所有数据集组合的相对频率；2) 使用Leaky Integrate and Fire (LIF) 神经元实现数据编码和组合生成策略；3) 将生成的组合输入恒定深度的阈门种群计数电路；4) 针对有限扇入和可变精度等硬件限制，设计了对数深度、对数立方线性连接数的种群计数电路，由无界扇入恒定深度阈门电路组合而成。

Result: 运行时间仅由待计算的组合数决定，无额外复杂度开销；空间复杂度为对数线性（log-linear）；种群计数电路在对数深度下实现，连接数为对数立方线性。

Conclusion: 所提出的阈门电路方案在计算上位性检测时优于传统计算方法，验证了神经形态硬件在生物信息学复杂组合问题中的应用潜力。

Abstract: The development of large-scale neuromorphic hardware has made practical implementations of threshold gate-based circuits a near-term possibility. The complexity advantages regarding traditional computing classes, as evidenced in the literature, have prompted us to tackle Epistasis Detection, one of the most computationally complex combinatorial problems in bioinformatics. We propose specially designed circuits that calculate the relative frequencies of all dataset combinations in an efficient pipelined fashion, taking advantage of co-located memory and configurable parallelism, obtaining complexity gains. Overall, we obtain the runtime to be bounded by the number of combinations to calculate, without any additional complexity overhead, contrary to classical approaches, using log-linear space. To accomplish this, we propose a data encoding and combination generation strategy using Leaky Integrate and Fire (LIF) neurons, that feeds a constant depth threshold gate population count circuit. Accounting for typical hardware characteristics, such as limited fan-in and variable precisions, we obtain logarithmic depth and log-cubic linear connections, for the population count circuit by composing developed unbounded fan-in constant depth threshold gate circuits to perform population count and binary array sum.

### [2] [Design-Oriented Modeling of TSV Substrate Noise Coupling to Ring VCOs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29867)
*Ilias Exouzidis,Alberto Garcia-Ortiz,George Floros,Georgios Panagopoulos*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种面向设计的紧凑型方法论，用于评估TSV（硅通孔）在混合信号电路中引起的衬底噪声。通过推导Signal-Ground TSV对的三端口RLGC宏模型，并在22 nm FD-SOI工艺的三级环形VCO上验证，结果表明TSV攻击者在1 GHz、0.5 Vpp下会引入-35.2 dBc的边带杂散，且耦合呈现低通特性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: TSV的金属-氧化物-硅结构会引入显著的寄生耦合路径，影响敏感RF模块的频谱纯度，因此需要一种设计导向的方法来评估TSV引起的衬底噪声。

Method: 推导了Signal-Ground TSV对的闭式解析三端口RLGC宏模型，显式暴露衬底节点；在22 nm FD-SOI工艺的三级环形VCO上进行验证，利用PDK中的RF器件直接访问晶体管衬底端子进行受控噪声注入，并通过Spectre RF的多音谐波平衡仿真量化TSV攻击者对振荡器输出频谱的影响。

Result: 1 GHz、0.5 Vpp的TSV攻击者引入-35.2 dBc的主边带杂散；杂散幅度随攻击者幅度单调增加；频率扫描显示低通耦合响应，杂散幅度从500 MHz的-20.2 dBc降至2 GHz的-33.1 dBc。

Conclusion: 该方法论能有效评估TSV引起的衬底噪声，且TSV耦合呈现低通特性，为3D-IC和chiplet系统中的RF设计提供了噪声评估依据。

Abstract: Through-silicon vias (TSVs) enable dense vertical interconnects in 3D-IC and chiplet systems, but their metal-oxide-silicon structure introduces significant parasitic coupling paths that can degrade the spectral purity of sensitive RF blocks. This paper presents a compact, design-oriented methodology for assessing TSV-induced substrate noise in mixed-signal circuits. We derive a closed-form analytical three-port RLGC macromodel for a Signal-Ground TSV pair that explicitly exposes the substrate node. The methodology is validated using a three-stage Ring VCO designed in a 22 nm FD-SOI technology, where specific RF devices from the process design kit (PDK) provide direct access to the transistor substrate terminals for controlled noise injection. Multi-tone Harmonic Balance simulations in Spectre RF quantify the impact of TSV aggressors on the oscillator's output spectrum. The results indicate that an aggressor of 1 GHz, 0.5 V$_{pp}$ induces a primary sideband spur of -35.2 dBc. Sensitivity characterization reveals that the magnitude of these sideband spurs increases monotonically with the aggressor amplitude. Furthermore, frequency sweeps demonstrate a low-pass coupling response, where the induced spur magnitude decreases from -20.2 dBc at 500 MHz to -33.1 dBc at 2 GHz.

### [3] [Precomputed 1D-CNNs for Atrial Fibrillation Detection on Tiny Smart Sensor Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29994)
*Lukas Einhaus,Natalie Maman,Julian Hoever,Andreas Erbslöh,Gregor Schiele*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于分组卷积的LUT预计算方法，用于在FPGA上高效部署1D-CNN进行时间序列分析。在MIT-BIH ECG数据集上实现房颤预测，仅用2,844个LUT、无DSP/BRAM，F1分数达95%。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的LUT预计算方法虽然能实现超低延迟和高资源效率，但可扩展性差。此前工作采用深度可分离卷积提升扩展性，但仍有改进空间，需要进一步泛化到更广泛的分组卷积形式。

Method: 1）将LUT预计算从深度可分离卷积推广到多种分组卷积形式；2）提出一种新型卷积块结构；3）设计算法指导该卷积块的超参数选择。

Result: 在AMD Spartan 7 S15 FPGA上部署，仅消耗2,844个LUT，无需DSP和BRAM，在MIT-BIH房颤预测数据集上F1分数达到95%。

Conclusion: 所提出的广义分组卷积LUT预计算方法有效提升了可扩展性，能在极低资源开销下实现高精度的时间序列分类，适合微小智能传感器系统。

Abstract: 1D-CNNs play a crucial role for time-series analysis on tiny smart sensor systems, e.g. for biosignal analysis, predictive maintenance, or structural health monitoring. LUTbased precomputation has emerged as an interesting optimization technique to implement such neural networks on FPGAs. The core idea is to precompute all possible outputs of a neural network layer and store them directly in the lookup tables of the FPGAs. This enables highly resource-efficient networks with ultra-low latency but suffers from poor scalability. Previous work has explored using depthwise-separable convolutions to improve scalability. In this paper, we generalize this approach to consider additional forms of grouped convolutions. Based on this, we propose a novel type of convolutional block and an algorithm to guide the choice of hyper parameters for this block. We evaluate our approach on a medical time-series dataset for predicting atrial fibrillation using the MIT-BIH database (ECG recordings). The resulting hardware accelerators are small enough to be deployed on an AMD Spartan 7 S15. They achieve a F1-Score of up to 95% while only requiring 2,844 LUTs and no DSPs or BRAM.

### [4] [elasticAI.explorer: Towards a Unified End-to-End Framework for Hardware-Aware Neural Architecture Search](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30019)
*Natalie Maman,Florian Hettstedt,Andreas Erbslöh,Gregor Schiele*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: elasticAI.explorer是一个基于Optuna的硬件感知NAS框架，通过YAML定义搜索空间、支持多种搜索空间类型、集成硬件代码生成和Docker交叉编译，实现自动化硬件在环NAS流程，降低嵌入式AI部署的工程开销。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有NAS框架将搜索空间定义、模型实现和部署管线紧密耦合，难以扩展到新硬件平台和自定义算子，工程开销大。

Method: 基于Optuna构建，采用YAML定义搜索空间并动态转换为可执行模型；支持层-wise、基于细胞和层次化搜索空间；集成硬件代码生成、Docker交叉编译工具链和自动创建设备基准测试二进制文件；提供可扩展的评估器（FLOPs、参数量、延迟估计）。

Result: 实现了一个统一的NAS框架，支持硬件在环工作流，减少嵌入式AI部署的工程负担，加速异构加速器平台的硬件感知NAS研究。

Conclusion: elasticAI.explorer通过解耦搜索空间定义与实现、集成硬件部署工具链，有效降低了NAS在嵌入式平台上的应用门槛，为异构加速器上的硬件感知NAS研究提供了可扩展的基础设施。

Abstract: Neural Architecture Search (NAS) has become an important approach for automatically designing neural networks under task-specific and hardware-specific constraints. However, many existing NAS frameworks tightly couple search space definitions, model implementations, and deployment pipelines, making extension to new hardware platforms and custom operators difficult. In this paper, we present the elasticAI.explorer, an extensible Python framework for hardware-aware NAS built on top of Optuna. The framework introduces a YAML-based search space specification that dynamically translates into executable neural network models during sampling. The approach supports layer-wise, cell-based, and hierarchical search spaces while maintaining a unified interface for optimization and deployment. Beyond architecture generation, the framework integrates hardware-specific code generation, Docker-based cross-compilation toolchains, and automated creation of on-device benchmarking binaries, enabling hardware-in-the-loop NAS workflows. The system further provides extensible evaluators for FLOPs, parameter count, and latency estimation. The elasticAI.explorer aims to reduce the engineering overhead of embedded AI deployment and accelerate research on hardware-aware NAS for heterogeneous accelerator platforms
