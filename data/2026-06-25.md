### [1] [SPORT: Spherical-PSNR-Optimized tRuncaTion for Power-Efficient 360-Degree Video Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24916)
*Md. Sajjad Hossain,Hasibur Rahman Hemel,Kyle Mooney,Yiwen Xu,William Oswald,Mario Renteria-Pinon,Hritom Das,Zhenlin Pei,Jinhui Wang,Na Gong*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: SPORT是一种面向VR头显的比特截断框架，通过仅存储视野外像素的最高有效位来降低显示路径内存功耗，在保持高质量感知的同时实现约48-52%的功耗节省，并已在130nm CMOS ASIC上验证。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: VR头显中内存带宽占总功耗30-40%，但现有系统对所有360度帧采用统一分辨率存储，未考虑用户注视点差异，导致大量不必要的功耗浪费。

Method: 提出SPORT框架：1) 基于WS-PSNR的比特截断优化，消除PSNR与WS-PSNR之间的度量不一致；2) 注视预测性瓦片分类补偿9.33ms端到端延迟，减少边界误分类5.2个百分点仅耗时0.01ms；3) SPORT-B（FoV无损）和SPORT-A（全自适应）两种变体。

Result: SPORT-B实现47.9%内存功耗节省和带宽降低，SSIM=1.000；SPORT-A达51.6%功耗节省，比PSNR优化器高3.1个百分点；TrunMEM360 ASIC验证字节级硅-软件一致性；DRAM泄漏降低48.72%，读写能耗降低36.4%/36.7%；总延迟9.33ms，满足20ms VR舒适预算。

Conclusion: SPORT通过注视感知的比特截断策略，在保持视觉质量的同时大幅降低VR头显内存功耗，并通过ASIC流片验证了方案的可行性和准确性，为下一代低功耗VR系统提供了实用解决方案。

Abstract: Memory bandwidth accounts for 30-40% of total power consumption in standalone virtual reality (VR) headsets, yet existing systems typically store the entire 360-degree frame at a uniform resolution regardless of viewer gaze. This paper presents SPORT (Spherical-PSNR Optimized tRuncaTion), a bit-truncation framework that reduces display-path memory power by storing only the most significant bits of pixels outside the user's field of view (FoV). Specifically, a new bit-truncation framework is developed to use weighted-to-spherically-uniform PSNR (WS-PSNR) directly in the optimization constraint, eliminating the metric inconsistency that arises when standard PSNR is used for a WS-PSNR quality target. Also, gaze-predictive tile classification compensates for the 9.33 ms end-to-end pipeline latency, reducing boundary misclassifications by 5.2 percentage points at a cost of only 0.01 ms. In addition, the developed SPORT-B variant, which keeps the FoV lossless, achieves 47.9% memory power saving and 47.9% bandwidth reduction across different 4K video sequences while satisfying all three per-region WS-PSNR thresholds and maintaining SSIM = 1.000 in the attended region. The full adaptive variant SPORT-A reaches 51.6% power saving, 3.1percentage points more than a PSNR-based optimizer at equal measured quality. SPORT is validated on the TrunMEM360 flexible SRAM Application-Specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC) fabricated in SkyWater 130 nm CMOS, confirming byte-exact silicon-software agreement, with WS-PSNR and SSIM matching within 0.1 dB and 0.001. CACTI-based analysis confirms 48.72% DRAM leakage reduction and 36.4%/36.7% read/write energy reduction. The total motion-to-photon latency of 9.33 ms satisfies the 20 ms VR comfort budget with a 53.3% safety margin.

### [2] [SafeGen: LLM-Driven Assertion Generation and Fault Criticality Evaluation for Functional Safety](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.25296)
*Xuanyi Tan,Arjun Chaudhuri,Rubin Parekhji,Krishnendu Chakrabarty*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: SafeGen是一个结合大语言模型和形式化验证的框架，用于汽车芯片功能安全中故障关键性评估，通过超知识图谱和功能安全断言实现比传统方法更优的语义可解释性与断言质量。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的基于仿真的故障分析在模块层面过于保守，无法准确反映故障关键性，需要更精确的语义级评估方法。

Method: 利用大语言模型(LLM)和文档级超知识图谱(HyperKG)集成FMEDA指南，结合RTL信息生成功能安全断言(FSA)，通过门级到RTL的故障映射机制和形式化属性验证(FPV)实现语义级故障关键性分级。

Result: 相比已有的LLM断言生成框架，SafeGen生成更高质量的断言；与传统仿真方法相比，在故障关键性评估中提供更强的语义可解释性。

Conclusion: SafeGen有效结合LLM与形式化验证，提升了汽车芯片功能安全评估的准确性和可解释性。

Abstract: With advances in autonomous driving and electric vehicle technologies, functional safety has become a critical requirement in automotive chip design. Traditional simulation-based fault analysis is often overly conservative at the module level and fails to accurately reflect fault criticality. This paper presents SafeGen, an LLM-driven, formal-verification-assisted framework for functional-safety-oriented fault criticality assessment. SafeGen leverages large language models (LLMs) and a document-level Hyper Knowledge Graph (HyperKG) that incorporates Failure Modes, Effects, and Diagnostic Analysis (FMEDA) guidelines to extract verifiable specifications from design and safety documents and evaluate their relevance to overall system safety. The HyperKG is further enriched with register-transfer-level (RTL) information to guide the generation of Functional Safety Assertions (FSAs) that are both semantically grounded and design-aware. Each assertion is linked to its corresponding specification, enabling traceable reasoning throughout the assessment process. A gate-to-RTL fault-mapping mechanism supporting both stuck-at and bridging faults, combined with formal property verification (FPV), enables semantic-level fault criticality grading based on specification-linked assertion violations. A digital-physical co-simulation platform for a field-oriented control (FOC) system is developed to validate SafeGen. Experimental results demonstrate that SafeGen generates higher-quality assertions than existing LLM-based assertion generation frameworks while providing greater semantic interpretability in fault criticality assessment compared with traditional simulation-based approaches.

### [3] [Cache-Resident LLM Inference in GB-Scale Last-Level Caches](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.25353)
*Wanning Zhang,Tongzhou Gu,Marco Canini,Ceyu Xu,Jian Weng*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种面向层次化内存集群系统的缓存驻留执行模型，将权重中心算子与注意力/KV缓存分离到专用资源域，通过解耦状态管理和依赖感知协调，在商用CPU上实现了2.04x-11.51x的TPOT加速比。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: LLM推理日益受限于数据在内存层次结构中的移动。尽管3D堆叠缓存技术已在现代服务器CPU中实现了GB级末级缓存，使得模型权重可片上驻留并利用缓存带宽，但更深层次的流水线会增加在途请求和KV缓存占用，且算子边界同步成为可见瓶颈。

Method: 提出缓存驻留执行模型：1) 将权重中心算子与注意力/KV缓存管理分离到专用资源域；2) 保持可复用权重缓存驻留，独立于流水线深度扩展KV容量；3) 将同步从算子边界放松到真正的子算子依赖关系。在多路CPU集群上实例化为权重-注意力解耦架构、局部性感知放置和专用静态运行时。

Result: 在Llama-3.2-3B和Llama-2-7B上实现2.04x-11.51x TPOT加速；分析模型预测最高可达13.9x加速。优于同等配置的llama.cpp。

Conclusion: 商用CPU配备GB级末级缓存时，通过以缓存驻留为中心、解耦状态管理和依赖感知协调的执行组织方式，可以高效支持LLM推理。

Abstract: Large language model (LLM) inference is increasingly dominated by data movement across the memory hierarchy. Recent 3D-stacked cache technologies have enabled GB-scale last-level caches in modern server CPUs, making it possible to keep reusable model weights on chip and exploit cache bandwidth and latency. Achieving this regime is not straightforward: deeper pipelining for weight residency increases in-flight requests and KV-cache footprint, while cache-resident operators make operator-boundary synchronization a visible bottleneck.
  We present a cache-resident execution model for inference on hierarchical-memory clustered systems. The model separates weight-centric operators from attention and KV-cache management into dedicated resource domains, keeping reusable weights cache-resident while scaling KV capacity independently of pipeline depth. It also relaxes synchronization from operator boundaries to true sub-operator dependencies, reducing coordination overhead in the cache-resident regime.
  We instantiate this model on a multi-socket CPU cluster with a weight-attention decoupled architecture, locality-aware placement, and a specialized static runtime. The prototype substantially outperforms equally provisioned llama.cpp. On deployed Llama-3.2-3B and Llama-2-7B configurations, it achieves 2.04x-11.51x speedup on time-per-output-token (TPOT). Under a validated analytical model, it further reaches up to 13.9x TPOT speedup across model sizes, context lengths, and batch sizes. These results show that commodity CPUs with GB-scale last-level caches can support efficient LLM inference when execution is organized around cache residency, decoupled state management, and dependency-aware coordination.

### [4] [Energy-Efficient CNN Acceleration with MSDF Digit-Serial Arithmetic on FPGA](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.25562)
*Muhammad Usman,Yousef Sadegheih,Dorit Merhof*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出一种面向U-Net图像分割的FPGA硬件加速器，采用合并乘加(MMA)流水线架构解决MSDF数字串行算术的初始延迟累积问题，在较低频率下实现相比CPU约7.8倍能效提升(15.14 GOPS/W vs 1.93 GOPS/W)，相比MSDF方案降低约9倍能耗。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: MSDF数字串行算术在级联操作(如乘法后加法)中存在初始延迟累积问题，每个单元引入独立启动开销，降低吞吐量。需要一种方法消除或减少这种延迟，提升能效。

Method: 提出合并乘加(MMA)架构，将乘法与加法融合为统一流水线，每个迭代仅引入一次精简延迟(远小于传统级联单元的延迟之和)，并采用空间输入深度并行处理机制。

Result: FPGA加速器在较低频率下获得15.14 GOPS/W能效(CPU为1.93 GOPS/W，约7.8倍提升)；相比MSDF方案降低约9倍能耗。

Conclusion: 合并算术方法能有效解决延迟累积问题，适用于资源受限、延迟敏感的医疗图像与计算机视觉边缘应用。

Abstract: This paper presents an energy-efficient hardware acceleration of the convolutional layers in the U-Net architecture for image segmentation, implemented on FPGA. While digit-serial arithmetic, particularly most-significant-digit-first (MSDF) techniques, offers a compact hardware footprint, it suffers from initial latency before producing the first output digit. This delay accumulates in cascaded operations like multiplication followed by addition, where each unit introduces its own startup overhead. To overcome this, we propose a merged multiply-add (MMA) architecture that fuses these operations into a unified pipeline. Instead of incurring separate delays, the MMA introduces a single streamlined latency per iteration, shorter than the combined latency of conventional cascaded units, resulting in enhanced throughput and efficiency. The MMA units are designed to process spatial input depths in parallel, achieving significantly higher performance than both standalone MSDF-based and conventional designs. We evaluate the proposed design using U-Net as a target application. Despite operating at a lower frequency than a CPU, the FPGA-based accelerator achieves up to an order of magnitude higher energy efficiency, delivering up to $15.14$ GOPS/W compared to $1.93$ GOPS/W for CPU-based inference. The design also shows approximately $9\times$ reduction in energy consumption compared to MSDF-based FPGA implementations. These results highlight the efficacy of the merged arithmetic approach for resource-constrained, latency-sensitive edge applications in medical imaging and computer vision.

### [5] [Croc: Training the Next Generation Chip Designers on Domain-Specific End-to-End Open Source Silicon](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.25673)
*Enrico Zelioli,Philippe Sauter,Thomas Benz,Hannah Pochert,Luisa Wüthrich,Beat Muheim,Frank K. Gürkaynak,Luca Benini*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一套基于开源RISC-V平台Croc的端到端SoC设计与流片流程，并在课程中成功验证：65名学生完成33个项目，30个产生可制造版图，18个入选流片候选，5个成功流片，首个芯片已通过硅验证，性能指标与使用闭源工具的同类产品相当。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前VLSI课程严重依赖受限制的EDA工具、PDK和闭源硬件设计，难以培养学生在架构设计到硅片制造的全流程动手能力，急需一套开源、可自由访问的端到端SoC设计与流片解决方案。

Method: 基于开源RISC-V平台Croc，集成开源SystemVerilog IP模块，配合130nm开源PDK，构建端到端开源设计流程；支持指令集扩展、加速协处理器和外设等多种领域定制选项，并首次在课程中大规模部署验证。

Abstract: The demand for domain-specific systems-on-chip (SoCs) in artificial intelligence, robotics, and automotive systems is increasing the need for engineers with hands-on expertise on very-large-scale integration (VLSI) design from architecture specification to fabricated silicon. Yet, most VLSI courses rely on restrictively licensed electronic design automation tools and process design kits (PDKs), as well as closed-source hardware designs. We present an end-to-end open-source domain-specific SoC design and fabrication flow built around Croc, a highly customizable RISC-V platform. Built from open-source SystemVerilog intellectual property blocks and integrated with an end-to-end open-source design flow in a 130nm open PDK, Croc enables tapeout projects supporting multiple domain customization options: instruction-set extensions, accelerator co-processors, and peripherals. In our first open-source course experience using Croc, 65 students completed 33 projects, 30 of which produced manufacturable layouts. 18 designs were selected as tapeout candidates, and five were fabricated. A first baseline chip has already been successfully characterized in silicon, demonstrating microcontroller-class functionality and implementation metrics comparable to those of products with similar functional complexity completed with closed-source toolchains and PDKs.
