### [1] [Field-Deployable RF Capture System for Indoor, Outdoor, and Foliage Environments](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01368)
*Lawrence Obiuwevwi,Krzysztof J. Rechowicz,Vikas Ashok,Sachin Shetty,Peter B. Foytik,Jared Cochran,Jeff Bobrow,Sampath Jayarathna*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于HackRF One、Raspberry Pi 5和GNSS的便携式电池供电RF采集系统，能在20 Msps采样率下记录带地理标签的IQ数据，并在 foliage、城市和室内环境中验证了其可靠性和实用性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有RF测量平台要么是实验室级且成本高昂，要么依赖固定基础设施，难以用于快速、分布式或长时段的现场测量，因此需要一种低成本、便携且高可靠性的方案。

Method: 集成HackRF One SDR、Raspberry Pi 5、GNSS接收器、稳压电池供电和高速固态存储，以SigMF格式记录连续IQ数据（最高20 Msps），并附带每段的位置和时间元数据。

Result: 在2.45 GHz的三类场景中： foliage场景信号接近底噪（-76至-82 dBFS）；城市场景显示30 dB动态范围的多径和WiFi信道重叠；室内场景WiFi占主导，建筑穿透损耗约20-25 dB，干扰底噪高出8-10 dB。系统写入吞吐75-85 MB/s，无丢样，GNSS同步<1秒，定位精度米级。

Conclusion: 该低成本便携SDR平台能够生成高质量、带地理标签的IQ数据集，适用于频谱表征、干扰分析、无线电环境地图构建及环境感知无线研究。

Abstract: Reliable and reproducible radio-frequency (RF) measurements in real-world environments are essential for characterizing spectrum behavior across unlicensed ISM and WiFi bands, licensed mid-band allocations, and emerging next-generation wireless deployments. Existing measurement platforms are often laboratory-grade, cost-prohibitive, or dependent on fixed infrastructure, limiting their practicality for rapid, distributed, or long-duration field campaigns. This paper presents a compact, battery-powered RF capture system integrating a HackRF One software-defined radio, Raspberry Pi 5, GNSS receiver, regulated battery supply, and high-speed solid-state storage. The platform records continuous IQ data at up to 20 Msps in SigMF format with per-segment location and timing metadata for reproducible spectrum analysis. Field experiments at 2.45 GHz in dense foliage, urban outdoor, and indoor office environments reveal distinct propagation signatures. Foliage measurements remain near the noise floor at -76 to -82 dBFS with limited spectral structure, consistent with strong canopy attenuation. Urban measurements show multipath activity across a 30 dB dynamic range, overlapping WiFi channels, and frequent ISM-band interference. Indoor measurements show dominant WiFi channels, an estimated 20 to 25 dB building entry loss relative to outdoor conditions, and an 8 to 10 dB higher interference floor caused by structural reflections. The system sustained 75 to 85 MB/s write throughput with no dropped samples or buffer underruns, while GNSS synchronization remained below one second with meter-level positioning. These results show that a portable, cost-effective SDR platform can produce high-fidelity, geotagged IQ datasets for spectrum characterization, interference analysis, radio environment mapping, and environment-aware wireless research.

### [2] [Physically-Aware Preemptive Virtual Channels for Deadlock-Free AXI Networks-on-Chip](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01430)
*Lorenzo Leone,Luca Colagrande,Luca Benini*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 针对多核SoC中AXI4协议在NoC上可能因读写流量依赖导致死锁的问题，本文评估了四种无死锁的流量分类方案，并提出了一种物理感知的Preemptive VCs架构，相比多平面设计可节省高达76%的链路资源，面积开销仅增3%。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 多核SoC规模扩展下，NoC需提供高内存带宽并确保无死锁。AXI4协议中读写流量间的协议级依赖会在网络端点形成循环等待，即使路由算法本身无死锁。解耦流量类可避免此类依赖，但存在实现权衡：多平面NoC会复制宽物理链路并增加路由压力，而传统VC路由器则引入大量控制复杂度、面积和时序开销。本文旨在重新审视这一权衡，寻求更优方案。

Method: 评估四种无死锁AXI4流量分类方案：一个多平面基线和三种轻量级基于VC的设计。其中提出Preemptive VCs（抢占式虚拟通道），一种物理感知架构，通过将链路资源复用与VC控制相结合，在减少物理链路数量的同时控制面积开销。

Result: Preemptive VCs相比多平面设计可节省高达76%的链路资源，频率相当，路由器面积开销仅增加3%。

Conclusion: 本文表明，在宽链路NoC中，通过精心设计的轻量级VC方案（如Preemptive VCs），可以在避免死锁的同时大幅降低链路资源开销，且面积代价极小，为未来多核SoC的NoC设计提供了有效的优化方向。

Abstract: As many-core Systems-on-Chip (SoCs) continue to scale, Networks-on-Chip (NoCs) must sustain increasingly high memory bandwidth while preserving deadlock freedom. In AXI4 systems, protocol-level dependencies between read and write traffic can create circular waits at the network endpoints, even when the routing algorithm itself is deadlock-free. Decoupling these traffic classes avoids such dependencies, but exposes a key implementation trade-off: multiplane NoCs duplicate wide physical links and increase routing pressure, whereas conventional Virtual Channel (VC) routers add substantial control complexity, area, and timing overhead. This work revisits this trade-off for modern wide-link NoCs. We evaluate four deadlock-free AXI4 traffic-class separation schemes: a multiplane baseline and three lightweight VC-based designs. Among these designs, we propose Preemptive VCs, a physically-aware architecture that can save up to 76% of link resources with comparable frequency and only 3% router area overhead relative to the multiplane design.

### [3] [MxGLUT: A Reconfigurable LUT-Centric Broadcast Dataflow Accelerator for Mixed-Precision GEMM](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01607)
*Weiyu Zhou,Chen Ding,Mingyuan Liu,Liangyu Gan,Yukun Feng,Hao Jia,Haoming Chu,Lirong Zheng,Ning Ma,Yuxiang Huan*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 提出MxGLUT，一种基于可重构LUT中心广播数据流的混合精度加速器，统一处理FP8-INT4和FP8-FP8矩阵运算，消除专用FP数据路径，在预填充和解码阶段均实现显著能效和面积效率提升。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有LUT加速器仅支持FP8-INT4且依赖独立FP数据路径处理注意力GEMM，导致硬件冗余和混合精度执行不统一；同时静态数据流无法适应预填充和解码阶段的不同计算特征。

Method: 提出MxGLUT架构，核心包括：(1) 基于混合精度LUT处理单元(MxLPE)的统一LUT计算框架，无需专用FP乘法器；(2) 可重构LUT中心广播(RLB)数据流，预填充阶段本地化部分和累加，解码阶段利用权重复用。

Result: 28nm CMOS下，乘法器面积减少56.92%，功耗在FP8-INT4和FP8-FP8模式下分别降低77.07%和78.35%；加速器面积效率0.492 TFLOPS/mm²，能效11.58 TFLOPS/W；支持FP8-FP8仅损失2.57%面积效率和3.34%能效；Llama系列上预填充和解码延迟分别加速2.16倍和1.49倍，能耗降至0.44倍和0.71倍，困惑度增加不超过1.70%。

Conclusion: MxGLUT通过统一的LUT计算机制和可重构数据流，有效解决了LLM推理中混合精度计算和不同阶段数据流不匹配的问题，在极小精度损失下实现了显著的硬件效率提升。

Abstract: Large language model (LLM) inference suffers from growing inefficiency across the prefill and decode phases, especially under weight-only quantization, where activations remain in FP8 while weights are compressed to low-bit integers. Existing LUT-based accelerators mainly target FP8-INT4 computation and still rely on separate floating-point (FP) datapaths for attention GEMM operations, leading to redundant hardware and non-unified mixed-precision execution. Moreover, their static dataflows are poorly matched to the distinct prefill and decode phases. To address these challenges, we propose MxGLUT, a reconfigurable LUT-centric broadcast (RLB) dataflow accelerator built on mixed-precision LUT-based processing elements (MxLPEs). Guided by a unified LUT-based execution framework, MxGLUT organizes both FP8-INT4 and FP8-FP8 GEMMs under a single LUT-based compute mechanism without dedicated FP multipliers or additional FP datapaths, and further adopts the RLB dataflow that localizes heavy partial-sum accumulation during the prefill phase and exploits weight reuse in the decode phase. Synthesized in UMC $28\,\mathrm{nm}$ CMOS at $200~\mathrm{MHz}$, MxGLUT reduces multiplier area by up to $56.92\%$ and power by up to $77.07\%$ and $78.35\%$ in FP8-INT4 and FP8-FP8 modes, respectively. At the accelerator level, MxGLUT achieves an area efficiency of $0.492~\mathrm{TFLOPS/mm^2}$ and an energy efficiency of $11.58~\mathrm{TFLOPS/W}$, while adding native FP8-FP8 support incurs only $2.57\%$ and $3.34\%$ reductions in area and energy efficiency, respectively, relative to the FP8-INT4-only FIGLUT baseline. Across the Llama family, MxGLUT achieves up to $2.16\times$ and $1.49\times$ latency speedup, and reduces normalized energy to $0.44\times$ and $0.71\times$ in prefill and decode, respectively, with at most $1.70\%$ perplexity increase.

### [4] [3DLS: A 3D Logic-Stacked Architecture for Disaggregated LLM Serving](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01617)
*Jaehun Lee,In-Jun Jung,Joo-Young Kim*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出3DLS，一种逻辑堆叠的3D芯片架构，通过垂直互连分离KV缓存传输与张量并行集合通信，解决PD分离LLM服务中混合流量争用导致的延迟问题，最高提升1.49倍吞吐量并降低60.2%端到端延迟。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有PD分离结合张量并行的芯片架构中，KV缓存传输与decode侧TP集合通信共享同一横向D2D互连，产生混合流量争用，增加通信延迟并恶化服务性能。

Method: 提出3DLS逻辑堆叠3D芯片架构，通过垂直互连路由KV缓存传输，同时将decode侧TP集合通信保留在横向D2D互连上，实现流量类别的物理隔离。

Result: 相比共享互连平面基线，吞吐量提升1.49倍，端到端延迟降低60.2%；相比工作负载感知优先级管理平面基线，吞吐量提升1.17倍，端到端延迟降低31.4%。

Conclusion: 物理隔离是未来基于芯片的PD分离LLM服务系统的重要设计原则，3D堆叠架构能有效分离不同通信流量，显著提升服务性能。

Abstract: Large language model (LLM) serving increasingly combines prefill-decode (PD) disaggregation with tensor parallelism (TP) to support large models and long contexts. In conventional 2D/2.5D chiplet architectures, layer-wise prefill-to-decode KV-cache transfer decode-side TP collectives share the same lateral die-to-die (D2D) interconnect, creating mixed-traffic contention on the decode critical path. This contention increases communication latency, prolongs token generation intervals, and degrades end-to-end serving performance. We propose 3DLS, a logic-on-logic 3D-stacked chiplet architecture that separates traffic classes by routing KV-cache transfers through vertical interconnects while preserving decode-side TP collectives on the lateral D2D fabric. 3DLS achieves up to 1.49$\times$ throughput and 60.2\% lower end-to-end (E2E) latency over the shared-fabric planar baseline, and still achieves up to 1.17$\times$ throughput and 31.4\% lower E2E latency over a workload-aware priority-managed planar baseline. These results highlight that physical isolation is an important design principle for future chiplet-based PD-disaggregated LLM serving systems.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Existing implementations often rely on CORDIC pipelines or BRAM-based look-up tables, increasing area and power consumption. This paper presents a BRAM-free approximate attention-weighting unit for FPGA-based ViT inference. The proposed design approximates the natural exponential in softmax using a 16-segment piecewise-linear function implemented entirely with distributed LUT fabric. Unlike base-2 approximations, the natural-exponential formulation preserves the pre-trained attention temperature and avoids model-specific recalibration. Implemented on a Xilinx Zynq-7020, the complete attention-row core uses 1444 LUTs, 77 DSPs, and no BRAM, while hardware-accurate emulation shows accuracy within a \(0.20\%\) absolute top-1 difference from the exact-softmax reference on ViT-family models. These results demonstrate the potential of the proposed core for energy-efficient ViT inference on resource-constrained edge-AI platforms.

### [6] [Probabilistic Memory for Trustworthy Edge Intelligence](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02465)
*Likai Pei,Jiahao Zheng,Xueji Zhao,Emilie Ye,Jianbo Liu,Hanqing Tao,Ming-Yen Lee,Ruiyang Qin,Yiyu Shi,Shimeng Yu,X. Sharon Hu,Ningyuan Cao*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出概率内存(p-MEM)，一种将分布参数存储并直接以原生内存带宽采样的统一存储原语，在CPU/GPU系统中实现超过1000 GSa/s/mm²的GRNG吞吐量，大幅降低指令数、采样延迟和能耗，为可信概率AI提供可扩展硬件基础。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 概率计算对可信边缘智能至关重要，但高斯随机数生成(GRNG)与计算之间存在数量级的数据吞吐量差距，且指令开销高，限制了其应用。

Method: 提出p-MEM统一存储原语，存储均值、标准差等分布参数，直接以原生内存带宽采样，确定性数据为零方差的特例；通过布局验证的模拟器探索器件选择、存储规格和技术节点。

This paper introduces probabilistic memory (p-MEM), a unified memory primitive that stores distribution parameters, such as mean and standard deviation, and samples directly at the native memory bandwidth, where deterministic data becomes the zero-variance special case. Using a layout-validated p-MEM simulator, we comprehensively explore device choices, memory specifications, and technology nodes, showing that p-MEM can achieve more than 1000 GSa/s/mm^2 GRNG throughput, including memory-array access. Integrated into CPU/GPU systems, p-MEM reduces instruction count by up to 2.19x/4.37x, sampling latency by 562x/3.45x, and energy by 295.5x/3.53x for Bayesian neural network workloads, providing a scalable hardware substrate for trustworthy probabilistic AI.
