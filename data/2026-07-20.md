### [1] [RTL-Sequencer: Towards Scalable RTL Timing Prediction with the Sequence-based Paradigm](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15830)
*Ziyan Guo,Wenji Fang,Wenkai Li,Yuchao Wu,Shang Liu,Zhiyao Xie*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: RTL-Sequencer是一种新颖的基于序列的范式，通过广度优先遍历线性化逻辑锥并应用现代线性序列模型，实现了可扩展的RTL时序预测，相比现有方法有显著改进。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: RTL级别的精确时序预测是设计自动化中长期存在的挑战，现有基于图的方法存在感受野有限、复杂度过高、缺乏信号方向性等问题。

Method: 提出RTL-Sequencer序列化范式，通过广度优先遍历将逻辑锥线性化，应用现代线性序列模型，并集成四种协同技术：序列洗牌、双向建模、可微分建模、混合图-序列架构。

Result: 大量实验表明RTL-Sequencer在性能上显著超越现有最先进的基线方法。

Conclusion: RTL-Sequencer推进了早期阶段的时序优化，为RTL时序预测提供了新的序列化解决思路。

Abstract: Accurate timing prediction at the register-transfer level (RTL) is a longstanding challenge in design automation. Existing graph-based methods struggle with limited receptive fields, high complexity, and a lack of signal directionality. We present RTL-Sequencer, a novel sequence-based paradigm that enables scalable RTL timing prediction via linearizing logic cones by breadth-first traversal and applying modern linear sequence models. Furthermore, sequence models are customized by four synergistic techniques, including sequence shuffling, bidirectional modeling, differentiable modeling, and a hybrid graph-sequence architecture. Extensive experiments demonstrate significant improvements of RTL-Sequencer over state-of-the-art baselines, advancing early-stage timing optimization.

### [2] [DSTAR: Accelerating Diffusion Transformers via Spatial and Temporal Redundancy Reduction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15846)
*Chi Zhang,Jieru Zhao,Yu Feng,Chen Zhang,Quan Chen,Minyi Guo*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: DSTAR是一个软硬件协同设计框架，通过混合精度量化和稀疏注意力重用机制减少扩散Transformer（DiT）的空间和时间冗余，在典型DiT上实现高达7.33倍延迟加速和41.89倍能耗节省。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: DiT虽然广泛应用，但其多迭代推理过程导致性能低下和能耗高。现有加速方法主要关注减少相邻时间步之间的时间冗余，忽视了DiT的特定特征，导致精度下降或效率不高。

Method: 算法层面：1) 细粒度混合精度量化方法处理线性操作中的差异化激活；2) 稀疏注意力重用机制减少注意力层的冗余计算。架构层面：设计专用硬件加速器实现高延迟和能效。

Result: 在7种典型DiT上，相比NVIDIA A100 GPU实现最高7.33倍延迟加速和41.89倍能耗节省；相比SOTA加速器实现最高2.54倍延迟加速和3.68倍能耗节省，且无精度损失。

Conclusion: DSTAR通过软硬件协同设计，有效解决了DiT推理的效率和能耗问题，在不损失精度的情况下取得显著加速和节能效果。

Abstract: Diffusion Transformers (DiTs) have been widely used in many tasks, including image synthesis, video generation, and content editing. However, their multi-iteration inference process leads to performance inefficiency and high energy consumption. Existing acceleration methods primarily focus on reducing temporal redundancy between adjacent timesteps, but often overlook the specific features of DiTs. As a result, these approaches either suffer from great accuracy degradation or fail to achieve high efficiency.
  We present DSTAR, a software-hardware co-design framework that accelerates DiT inference by reducing spatial and temporal redundancy. At the algorithmic level, DSTAR introduces a fine-grained mixed-precision quantization method for differential activations in linear operations, significantly increasing the proportion of low-bit computations. Additionally, DSTAR incorporates a sparse attention reuse mechanism to minimize redundant computation in attention layers. For architectural support, we design a specialized hardware accelerator which achieves high efficiency in both latency and energy consumption. Evaluation on seven typical DiTs demonstrates that DSTAR achieves up to 7.33x latency speedup and 41.89x energy savings compared to an NVIDIA A100 GPU, and achieves up to 2.54x latency speedup and 3.68x energy savings compared to SOTA accelerators, without accuracy degradation.

### [3] [From Patterns to Parsers: Automatic Generation of Efficient Hardware Parsers for FPGAs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16058)
*Tushar Garg,Andrew Boutros*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种开源工具，能够从高级规范自动生成高效的硬件解析器。它采用解析中间表示（PIR）解耦前端与后端，后端生成优化的SystemVerilog，支持FSM生成、字节对齐、多周期字段跨越等功能。通过自定义符号令牌扩展模式匹配，支持范围验证、取反、外部端口比较等操作。在P4（以太网协议解析）和Snort（网络入侵检测）两个前端流程中验证，生成的以太网解析器频率提升高达226%，FPGA逻辑资源减少97%，层次化模式分解可减少8倍资源消耗。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有硬件解析器生成工具表达能力有限，仅支持简单的相等检查，且前端与后端紧耦合，难以复用。需要一种灵活、高效、开源的工具来支持多种应用场景（如协议解析、入侵检测）的硬件解析器自动生成。

Method: 提出解析中间表示（PIR）解耦前端和后端；后端自动处理FSM生成、字节对齐、多周期字段跨越；引入自定义符号令牌扩展模式匹配能力（范围验证、取反、外部比较）；支持P4和Snort两种前端流程，共享同一后端。

Result: 以太网解析器相比现有工作频率提升高达226%，FPGA逻辑资源减少97%；层次化模式分解相比单体设计减少8倍资源消耗。

Conclusion: 该开源框架能够帮助设计者快速实现高性能、资源高效、厂商无关的硬件解析器，适用于多种应用场景。

Abstract: This work presents an open-source tool for automatically generating efficient hardware parsers from high-level specifications. It uses a parsing intermediate representation (PIR) that decouples application-specific frontends from a common register-transfer level (RTL) generation backend. The backend produces optimized, human-readable SystemVerilog, handling FSM generation, byte-alignment, and multi-cycle field straddling for arbitrary datapath widths. The tool also extends pattern matching beyond simple equality checks by introducing custom symbolic tokens to support operations that existing parser generators cannot express, such as range validation, negation, and comparisons against external ports. We demonstrate two end-to-end flows using a P4 frontend for Ethernet protocol parsing and a Snort frontend for network intrusion detection, both using the same unmodified backend. The generated Ethernet parsers achieve up to 226% higher operating frequency and up to 97% fewer FPGA logic resources than prior work. A controlled synthetic study further shows that the tool's hierarchical pattern decomposition yields up to 8x resource utilization reduction over monolithic designs. Our open-source framework enables designers to rapidly implement high-performance, resource-efficient, vendor-agnostic hardware parsers for diverse applications.
