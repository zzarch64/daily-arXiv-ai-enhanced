### [1] [FlatAttention: Dataflow and Fabric Collectives Co-Optimization for Large Attention-Based Model Inference on Tile-Based Accelerators](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02110)
*Chi Zhang,Luca Colagrande,Renzo Andri,Luca Benini*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: FlatAttention是一种针对基于瓦片架构加速器的注意力机制数据流优化方案，通过利用片上网络原语减少HBM访问，显著提升利用率和性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 随着混合专家模型的发展，注意力计算在推理中占比越来越大，而新兴的芯片级/晶圆级瓦片架构加速器需要高效的数据流管理来减少内存瓶颈，提升利用率。

Method: 提出FlatAttention数据流，针对现代注意力变体在瓦片架构上的优化，利用片上网络集成的集体原语来最小化高带宽内存访问。

Result: 在32x32瓦片配置下，达到92.3%利用率，相比FlashAttention-3有4.1倍加速和16倍HBM流量降低；在DeepSeek-v3 FP8解码中实现1.9倍系统吞吐提升和1.4倍延迟降低。

Conclusion: FlatAttention为基于瓦片架构的加速器提供了高效的注意力数据流解决方案，能够显著提升现代注意力变体的计算效率和系统性能。

Abstract: Attention accounts for an increasingly dominant fraction of total computation during inference for mixture-of-experts (MoE) models, making efficient acceleration critical. Emerging domain-specific accelerators for large model inference are shifting toward chip-scale and wafer-scale tile-based architectures. Tiles contain large matrix and vector engines and are connected through on-chip interconnects, which support tile-to-tile traffic to reduce the tile-to-main-memory traffic bottleneck. Hence, dataflow management is crucial to achieve high utilization. We propose FlatAttention, a dataflow for modern attention variants on tile-based accelerators. FlatAttention minimizes expensive high-bandwidth memory (HBM) accesses by exploiting collective primitives integrated into the on-chip network fabric, achieving up to 92.3% utilization, 4.1x speedup over FlashAttention-3, and 16x lower HBM traffic. On a 32x32 tile configuration with peak performance comparable to NVIDIA GH200, FlatAttention generalizes across multiple attention variants, achieving an average of 86% utilization for compute-bound attentions and 78% HBM bandwidth utilization for memory-bound ones, resulting in an average 1.9x speedup over attention implementations on GH200. Finally, we evaluate end-to-end DeepSeek-v3 FP8 decoding with FlatAttention on a wafer-scale multi-die system, achieving a 1.9x improvement in system throughput and a 1.4x reduction in per-user token output latency, despite operating with 1.5x lower peak system performance compared to the state-of-the-art solution.

### [2] [TensorPool: A 3D-Stacked 8.4TFLOPS/4.3W Many-Core Domain-Specific Processor for AI-Native Radio Access Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02291)
*Marco Bertuletti,Yichao Zhang,Diyou Shen,Alessandro Vanelli-Coralli,Frank K. Gürkaynak,Luca Benini*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: TensorPool是一个为6G AI原生物理层设计的专用处理器，包含256个RISC-V核心和16个张量引擎，在TSMC N7工艺下实现高性能张量计算，相比纯核心集群性能提升6倍，能效提升9.1倍，3D堆叠技术进一步优化面积效率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 6G网络中AI原生物理层模型虽然能提升服务质量，但计算复杂度高，难以满足亚毫秒级实时约束和≤100W的功耗限制。需要专门针对AI-RAN的高吞吐量张量计算进行领域专用优化，实现能效和灵活性平衡。

Method: 设计TensorPool处理器集群：256个RISC-V32IMAF可编程核心，配备16个256 MACs/cycle（FP16）张量引擎，4MiB L1暂存器实现低延迟数据访问。采用TSMC N7工艺实现，并探索3D堆叠技术优化计算块与内存路由。

Result: 在AI-RAN张量操作中达到3643 MACs/cycle（89%张量单元利用率），相比无张量加速的核心集群性能提升6倍，GOPS/W/mm²能效提升9.1倍。3D堆叠实现2.32倍面积效率提升且无频率下降。

Conclusion: TensorPool通过领域专用设计成功解决了6G AI原生物理层的计算复杂度和能效挑战，为未来高密度、低功耗基站部署提供了可行的硬件解决方案，3D堆叠技术进一步提升了面积效率。

Abstract: The upcoming integration of AI in the physical layer (PHY) of 6G radio access networks (RAN) will enable a higher quality of service in challenging transmission scenarios. However, deeply optimized AI-Native PHY models impose higher computational complexity compared to conventional baseband, challenging deployment under the sub-msec real-time constraints typical of modern PHYs. Additionally, following the extension to terahertz carriers, the upcoming densification of 6G cell-sites further limits the power consumption of base stations, constraining the budget available for compute ($\leq$ 100W). The desired flexibility to ensure long term sustainability and the imperative energy-efficiency gains on the high-throughput tensor computations dominating AI-Native PHYs can be achieved by domain-specialization of many-core programmable baseband processors. Following the domain-specialization strategy, we present TensorPool, a cluster of 256 RISCV32IMAF programmable cores, accelerated by 16 256 MACs/cycle (FP16) tensor engines with low-latency access to 4MiB of L1 scratchpad for maximal data-reuse. Implemented in TSMC's N7, TensorPool achieves 3643~MACs/cycle (89% tensor-unit utilization) on tensor operations for AI-RAN, 6$\times$ more than a core-only cluster without tensor acceleration, while simultaneously improving GOPS/W/mm$^2$ efficiency by 9.1$\times$. Further, we show that 3D-stacking the computing blocks of TensorPool to better unfold the tensor engines to L1-memory routing provides 2.32$\times$ footprint improvement with no frequency degradation, compared to a 2D implementation.
