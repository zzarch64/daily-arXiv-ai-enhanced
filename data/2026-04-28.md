### [1] [Hardware-Efficient FPGA Implementation of Sigmoid Function Using Mixed-Radix Hyperbolic Rotation CORDIC](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.23547)
*Chintan Panchal,Ankur Changela,Mohendra Roy*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于混合基数CORDIC架构的硬件高效sigmoid激活函数FPGA实现方法，通过将sigmoid转换为tanh计算、采用混合基数旋转CORDIC算法及线性向量CORDIC，在Virtex-7 FPGA上仅用835个逻辑片、零DSP资源，实现了4.23×10^-4的平均绝对误差。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在资源受限的边缘设备（如FPGA）上高效部署人工神经网络时，非线性激活函数的硬件实现至关重要。sigmoid函数虽广泛用于概率输出、二分类和循环神经网络的门控机制，但其依赖指数运算，硬件实现成本高，因此需要一种资源高效的FPGA实现方案。

Method: 提出混合基数CORDIC架构：1) 将输入范围归一化到1，利用sigmoid与tanh的数学关系将tanh计算范围缩小至0.5；2) 采用改进的混合基数双曲旋转CORDIC（MR-HRC）算法，先以radix-2确保稳定收敛，再以radix-4加速收敛且无需比例因子补偿；3) 末级使用radix-2线性向量CORDIC（R2-LVC），通过MR-HRC输出的双曲正弦/余弦值计算tanh；4) 整体采用全流水线架构，16-bit定点数表示。

Result: 在Xilinx Virtex-7 FPGA上实现，仅消耗835个逻辑片，零DSP资源使用，平均绝对误差（MAE）为4.23×10^-4，优于多个近期sigmoid实现方案。

Conclusion: 提出的混合基数CORDIC架构在硬件资源消耗和计算精度之间取得了良好平衡，显著降低了硬件开销（零DSP使用），同时保持了高精度，适合资源受限的边缘计算场景。

Abstract: Efficient hardware implementation of nonlinear activation functions is a crucial task in deploying artificial neural networks on resource-constrained and edge devices such as Field-Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs). The sigmoid activation function is widely used for probabilistic output, binary classification, and gating mechanisms in recurrent neural networks, despite its reliance on exponential computations.
  This paper presents a hardware-efficient FPGA implementation of the sigmoid activation function using a mixed-radix CORDIC-based architecture. The proposed approach leverages the mathematical relationship between the sigmoid and hyperbolic tangent functions. The input range is normalized to 1, enabling the corresponding tanh computation to operate within a reduced range of 0.5, which significantly improves convergence behavior.
  To achieve high accuracy with minimal hardware overhead, a modified mixed-radix hyperbolic rotation CORDIC (MR-HRC) algorithm combining radix-2 and radix-4 iterations is introduced. The initial radix-2 stage ensures stable convergence, while the subsequent radix-4 stage accelerates convergence without requiring scale-factor compensation. In the final stage, a radix-2 linear vectoring CORDIC (R2-LVC) is used to compute the hyperbolic tangent by dividing hyperbolic sine and cosine values derived from the MR-HRC algorithm.
  The entire architecture is fully pipelined and implemented on an FPGA. The design is realized on an Xilinx Virtex-7 FPGA using a 16-bit fixed-point representation. Experimental results demonstrate a significant reduction in hardware utilization, requiring only 835 logic slices with zero DSP usage. Additionally, the design achieves a mean absolute error of 4.23 10^-4, outperforming several recent sigmoid implementations.

### [2] [TimingLLM: A Two-Stage Retrieval-Augmented Framework for Pre-Synthesis Timing Prediction from Verilog](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.23602)
*Armin Abdollahi,Negin Ashrafi,Mehdi Kamal,Massoud Pedram*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 提出TimingLLM，一个两阶段检索增强LLM流水线，直接从Verilog预测WNS和TNS，无需综合工具，速度比现有方法快1.3-1.6倍，准确率高（R_WNS=0.91, R_TNS=0.97）。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 早期、无需工具的时序预测是快速RTL迭代的关键障碍，现有方法依赖综合工具，迭代速度慢。

Method: 两阶段流水线：Stage1用微调LLM作为时序Oracle生成路径级信息并总结为结构时序线索；Stage2用LLM回归器预测WNS/TNS，并在最后一层应用基于k近邻的对角转向向量。

Result: 在VerilogEval上R_WNS=0.91（MAPE 12%），R_TNS=0.97（MAPE 16%），速度比先前方法快1.3-1.6倍。仅需1000个样本微调即可适配新工艺库和PVT角落。

Conclusion: TimingLLM实现了高效、可迁移的RTL级时序预测，将发布60k模块的训练语料，为快速设计空间探索提供了实用工具。

Abstract: Early, tool-free prediction of post-synthesis timing remains a key obstacle to rapid RTL iteration. We introduce TimingLLM, a two-stage retrieval-augmented LLM pipeline that estimates worst negative slack (WNS) and total negative slack (TNS) directly from Verilog. Stage 1 is a fine-tuned LLM that acts as a compact post-synthesis timing oracle, producing path-level arrivals/required times that are summarized into lightweight structural-timing cues (e.g., bag-of-gates counts, critical-path depth, gate-type patterns). Stage 2 is an LLM-based regressor that predicts WNS/TNS and applies a learned diagonal steering vector at the last transformer block, computed from the k nearest timing-labeled modules in a disjoint retrieval bank. On VerilogEval, TimingLLM attains R_WNS = 0.91 (MAPE 12%) and R_TNS=0.97 (MAPE 16%) while running 1.3-1.6 times faster than prior methods. Training uses a new 60k-module Verilog corpus with synthesis reports, which we will release. After training once, TimingLLM can be adapted to new technology libraries and PVT corners by refitting only a small regression head on 1000 labeled modules per setting, consistently outperforming state-of-the-art baselines.

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种针对边缘设备的硬件高效Softmax和LayerNorm设计，在保证归一化准确性的前提下，通过近似方法大幅降低硬件面积（Softmax面积942μm²，LayerNorm面积1199μm²），相比现有技术分别实现11倍和14倍的面积缩减，且在NLP任务上精度损失极小。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Transformer模型中非GEMM操作（如Softmax和LayerNorm）因非线性特性在边缘设备上占据大量硬件成本。现有近似方法主要适用于分类任务（面向排序），但边缘NLP和生成式AI更依赖分数导向任务，需要保证归一化正确性（Softmax求和为1，LayerNorm标准差为1）。

Method: 采用硬件高效的近似方法设计Softmax和LayerNorm，同时严格保证归一化约束。架构用Verilog HDL描述，基于三星28nm CMOS工艺综合实现。

Conclusion: 所提设计在极小面积开销下实现了可保证归一化的非线性操作，适合资源受限的边缘NLP和生成式AI场景。

Abstract: In Transformer models, non-GEMM (non-General Matrix Multiplication) operations -- especially Softmax and Layer Normalization (LayerNorm) -- often dominate hardware cost due to their nonlinear nature. To address this, previous approximation studies mainly target rank-oriented tasks, which is acceptable for classification. However, edge Natural Language Processing (NLP) applications and edge generative AI are largely evaluated based on score-oriented tasks, so normalization-guaranteed non-GEMM operations are essential. We propose a hardware-efficient Softmax and LayerNorm with Guaranteed Normalization for Edge devices. Our design employs hardware-efficient approximation methods while preserving the normalization (Softmax: $\sum p = 1$, LayerNorm: $σ= 1$). Our architecture is described in Verilog HDL and synthesized using the Samsung 28nm CMOS process. In accuracy evaluation, we achieve high accuracy with minimal degradation: GLUE +0.07%, SQuAD -0.01%, perplexity -0.09%. Implementation results show that our architecture is small: $942\,μm^2$ for Softmax, $1199\,μm^2$ for LayerNorm. Compared to the state of the art, we achieve up to 11x and 14x reduction in area, respectively.

TL;DR: 本文通过在同一硬件平台上对比GPU（TensorRT FP16）和CPU（ONNX Runtime FP32）执行路径，证明了模型即使满足准确性验证和安全性阈值超标率（STER=0），仍可能在部署负载下违反时序约束（如CPU路径延迟比GPU高9.8倍，超过10Hz临床周期预算65%），并提出联合STER与延迟验证的方法以符合FDA草案要求。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前上市前验证协议未在推理层对准确性、输出稳定性和时序约束进行独立验证，导致系统可能在满足前两者的前提下违反时序要求，存在安全隐患。

Method: 在相同硬件（NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano Super）上，对同一MobileNetV2模型分别部署于GPU加速路径（TensorRT FP16）与通用CPU路径（ONNX Runtime FP32），在相同对抗负载下测量STER和延迟指标。

Abstract: A system can satisfy accuracy-based validation, maintain output stability (Safety-Threshold Exceedance Rate, STER, equal to zero), and still violate timing constraints under deployment load. ### [7] [Déjà Vu Packing: Optimizing FPGA Logic Clustering Runtime via Pattern Memoization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24649)
*Milo Liebster,Amin Mohaghegh,Andrew Boutros*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 针对FPGA布局打包过程中昂贵且冗余的布线合法性检查问题，本文提出Déjà Vu打包方法，利用打包签名树数据结构识别重复打包模式并缓存检查结果，在AMD 7系列和Altera Stratix-10架构上实现平均3.7x和6.9x的打包加速，端到端VPR流程平均加速1.6x和5.3x，且不损失结果质量。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现代FPGA逻辑块架构日益复杂（包含复杂逻辑单元和高度不规则的局部互连），导致CAD工具在打包阶段需要解决昂贵的多源多汇布线问题以验证簇内布线可行性。实验表明，打包阶段的合法性检查消耗了VPR流程58%（7系列）和94%（Stratix-10）的运行时间，成为显著瓶颈。

Method: 提出Déjà Vu打包方法，核心创新是引入**打包签名树**（packing signature tree）数据结构。该结构高效地识别重复出现的打包模式，并对其合法性检查结果进行记忆化（memoization），从而跳过大量冗余的检查操作。

Conclusion: 本文证明了打包阶段是FPGA CAD流程的主要性能瓶颈，并展示了通过识别和复用打包模式来消除冗余计算的有效性。Déjà Vu方法在不牺牲结果质量的前提下显著提升了VPR整体流程的运行效率。

Abstract: Implementing a digital circuit on an FPGA fabric requires clustering technology-mapped netlist primitives into coarser-granularity blocks that can be directly mapped to the physical resources available on the FPGA. As the architecture of FPGA logic blocks (LBs) has grown in complexity, with sophisticated logic elements (LEs) and highly irregular local interconnect, this packing problem has become more challenging. To ensure the feasibility of intracluster routing, the computer-aided design (CAD) tools must solve a costly multi-source multi-sink routing problem for each candidate cluster. In this paper, we first show that such packing legality checks consume a significant portion of the CAD flow runtime for LB architectures with complex LEs and local routing structures resembling modern commercial FPGAs. By analyzing the packing algorithm behavior, we observe that a significant fraction of the attempted packed clusters are repetitions of a much smaller number of packing patterns, and therefore many of the packing legality checks are redundant and could be skipped. To this end, we introduce our Déjà Vu packing approach, which leverages a novel packing signature tree data structure that enables efficient identification of recurring packing patterns and memoization of their legality check outcomes. Our approach speeds up the packing by up to 13.4x and 29.3x, with an average of 3.7x and 6.9x, across the evaluated benchmarks on the 7-series and Stratix 10 architectures. These packing runtime gains result in a significant 1.6x and 5.3x average reduction in end-to-end VPR runtime, while maintaining quality of results.
