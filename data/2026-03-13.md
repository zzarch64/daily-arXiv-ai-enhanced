*Runbang Hu,Bo Fang,Bingzhe Li,Yuede Ji*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: VeriHGN：基于增强型异构图统一电路组件与空间网格的布局拥塞预测框架，在工业基准测试中优于现有方法

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 随着VLSI设计规模和复杂度增加，布局验证成为EDA流程中的核心挑战。传统方法只能在详细布线后准确识别拥塞，耗时且成本高。现有学习方法的电路连接性和布局特征建模松散，且主要产生数值拥塞估计，需要更紧密的建模方法。

Method: 提出VeriHGN验证框架，基于增强型异构图将电路组件和空间网格统一到单一关系表示中，实现对逻辑意图与物理实现之间交互的更忠实建模。

Conclusion: VeriHGN通过统一的关系表示有效建模电路逻辑与物理实现的交互，为早期拥塞预测提供了更准确的解决方案，减少了布线迭代需求。

Abstract: As Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) designs continue to scale in size and complexity, layout verification has become a central challenge in modern Electronic Design Automation (EDA) workflows. In practice, congestion can only be accurately identified after detailed routing, making traditional verification both time-consuming and costly. Learning-based approaches have therefore been explored to enable early-stage congestion prediction and reduce routing iterations. However, although prior methods incorporate both netlist connectivity and layout features, they often model the two in a loosely coupled manner and primarily produce numerical congestion estimates. We propose VeriHGN, a verification framework built on an enhanced heterogeneous graph that unifies circuit components and spatial grids into a single relational representation, enabling more faithful modeling of the interaction between logical intent and physical realization. Experiments on industrial benchmarks, including ISPD2015, CircuitNet-N14, and CircuitNet-N28, demonstrate consistent improvements over state-of-the-art methods in prediction accuracy and correlation metrics.

### [2] [Synthesis-in-the-Loop Evaluation of LLMs for RTL Generation: Quality, Reliability, and Failure Modes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11287)
*Weimin Fu,Zeng Wang,Minghao Shao,Ramesh Karri,Muhammad Shafique,Johann Knechtel,Ozgur Sinanoglu,Xiaolong Guo*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 该论文提出硬件质量指数(HQI)来评估语言模型生成的RTL代码质量，超越了传统仅关注功能正确性的评估方法，发现不同模型在可综合性和硬件效率方面存在显著差异。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有评估方法仅关注RTL代码的功能正确性，忽略了可综合性和实现质量这两个关键硬件设计指标，导致无法全面评估语言模型在硬件设计中的实际表现。

Method: 使用硬件质量指数(HQI)这一0-100分制的综合指标，结合综合后的面积、延迟和警告数量，在Nangate45 45nm工艺下评估32个语言模型在202个Verilog任务上的表现，每个任务尝试5次。

Result: 模型分为三个层级：13个前沿模型HQI超过71（Gemini-3-Pro最佳，覆盖率87.5%，HQI 85.1）；11个中阶模型HQI在53-68之间；8个模型低于53。专有模型和开源模型在综合失败模式上存在系统性差异。

Conclusion: RTL生成需要超越功能正确性的评估，HQI提供了全面的质量度量。模型在可综合性和硬件效率方面存在显著差异，多采样策略可以缩小能力与部署之间的差距，专有和开源模型的失败模式反映了不同的训练数据质量。

Abstract: RTL generation demands more than software code synthesis: designs must be syntactically valid, synthesizable, functionally correct, and hardware-efficient. Existing evaluations stop at functional correctness, leaving synthesizability and implementation quality unmeasured.
  We evaluate 32 language models on 202 Verilog tasks from VerilogEval and RTLLM, with five attempts each, scoring via the Hardware Quality Index (HQI), a 0--100 metric integrating post-synthesis area, delay, and warning count relative to expert references under a Nangate45 45\,nm flow. Three performance tiers emerge: 13 frontier models achieve Global HQI above 71, led by Gemini-3-Pro (87.5\% coverage, 85.1 HQI); 11 mid-tier models cluster at 53--68; 8 fall below 53. The capability-to-deployment gap (best-of-five vs.\ single-attempt) spans 3.8--22.1 HQI points, motivating multi-sample strategies. A tool-adjudicated taxonomy of 195 genuine synthesis failures reveals systematic divergence: proprietary models fail late through elaboration errors and synthesis timeout; open-weight models fail early through missing module wrappers and non-synthesizable constructs, consistent with training on simulation-grade rather than synthesis-grade RTL. Rankings hold across three technology libraries at Spearman~$ρ> 0.99$.

### [3] [Link Quality Aware Pathfinding for Chiplet Interconnects](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11612)
*Aaron Yen,Jooyeon Jeong,Puneet Gupta*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文开发了一个基于RTL综合的流程，用于评估小芯片间互连链路的纠错开销，并提出基于约束规划的系统级链路分配方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 随着小芯片集成技术的发展，设计师需要在多种短距离芯片间互连技术中进行选择，这些技术在带宽密度、能耗、距离和误码率等方面差异很大。在满足严格的误码率目标时，纠错码会带来能量、面积和吞吐量开销，需要系统化的评估方法。

Method: 开发了基于RTL综合功率和面积估计的流程，用于评估不同收发器误码率下的纠错开销。综合了参数化的Reed-Solomon码、CRC-64和Go-Back-N重传逻辑。提出了基于CP-SAT的链路分配公式，在距离、带宽和海岸线约束下使用纠错修正后的指标进行系统级优化。

Result: 结果显示，纠错码会显著改变基于常见性能指标的链路比较结果。在中等误码率下，CRC+ARQ可以减少所需的RS强度（和解码器开销），同时仍能满足严格的误码率目标。

Conclusion: 本文提出的流程和优化方法能够支持小芯片系统在严格误码率目标下的互连技术选择和系统级优化，为小芯片集成设计提供了有效的评估和优化框架。

Abstract: As chiplet-based integration advances, designers must select among short-reach die-to-die interconnect technologies with widely varying shoreline and areal bandwidth density, energy per bit, reach, and raw bit error rate (BER). Meeting stringent delivered BER targets in chiplet systems requires error-correcting codes (ECC), but incurs energy, area, and throughput overheads. We develop a flow centered around RTL synthesis power and area estimations to support pathfinding of inter-chiplet links under a stringent 10-27 delivered BER target. We synthesize a parameterized Reed-Solomon code with CRC-64 and Go-Back-N retry logic to estimate the correction overhead for different transceiver bit error rates. Results show that ECC can materially change link comparisons under common figures of merit and that CRC+ARQ can reduce the required RS strength (and decoder overhead) at moderate BERs while still meeting stringent delivered-BER targets. We present a CP-SAT-based link assignment formulation that uses these ECC-corrected metrics under reach, delivered-bandwidth, and shoreline constraints in system-level optimization.

### [4] [Implementing and Optimizing an Open-Source SD-card Host Controller for RISC-V SoCs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11849)
*Axel Vanoni,Philippe Sauter,Paul Scheffler,Anton Buchner,Micha Wehrli,Thomas Benz,Luca Benini*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出一个开源SD主机控制器接口，集成到Cheshire RISC-V SoC平台，通过优化驱动程序和避免不必要的fence指令，显著提升存储性能，达到11.1 MB/s吞吐量。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 开源RISC-V SoC需要高效的非易失性存储解决方案。SPI接口吞吐量有限，而SD卡是嵌入式Linux系统的标准存储介质，因此需要高效的SD主机控制器集成。

Method: 开发开源SD主机控制器接口(SDHCI)外设，集成到Cheshire RISC-V SoC平台。控制器和软件栈设计时充分考虑CVA6内存系统和Linux驱动行为，通过定制驱动寄存器访问路径和避免不必要的fence指令来优化性能。

Result: 完全开源控制器实现最高11.1 MB/s吞吐量，接近SD接口12.5 MB/s的理论极限，比基于SPI的存储方案提供高达6.5倍的吞吐量提升。

Conclusion: 成功开发并集成了开源SD主机控制器到RISC-V SoC平台，通过识别和解决由RISC-V内存模型和CVA6 fence指令实现导致的性能瓶颈，显著提升了存储性能，为开源RISC-V平台提供了实用的高效存储解决方案。

Abstract: Recent announcements have shown the viability of end-to-end open-source (OS) Linux-capable RISC-V systems on chip (SoCs). However, practical application and software development platforms require efficient non-volatile storage, which is not adequately served by common SPI-based interfaces due to their limited throughput. Secure Digital (SD) cards are the de facto standard storage medium for embedded Linux systems; efficient SD host controller (SDHC) integration is thus essential for open-source RISC-V platforms. We present an OS SD host controller interface (SDHCI) peripheral integrated into the end-to-end OS Cheshire RISC-V SoC platform. The controller and its software stack are designed with full awareness of CVA6's memory system and Linux driver behavior; during evaluation, we identify a significant performance bottleneck caused by the RISC-V memory model and CVA6's implementation of the fence instruction, which flushes the pipeline and data cache on memory-mapped register accesses when cache management operations (CMOs) are unavailable. By customizing the driver's register access paths and avoiding unnecessary fences, we substantially reduced this overhead. Our fully OS controller achieves up to 11.1 MB/s throughput, approaching the 12.5 MB/s limit of the SD interface and providing up to 6.5 times the throughput of SPI-based storage.

### [5] [SNAP-V: A RISC-V SoC with Configurable Neuromorphic Acceleration for Small-Scale Spiking Neural Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11939)
*Kanishka Gunawardana,Sanka Peeris,Kavishka Rambukwella,Thamish Wanduragala,Saadia Jameel,Roshan Ragel,Isuru Nawinne*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: SNAP-V：基于RISC-V的神经形态SoC，针对小规模SNN推理优化，包含两种加速器变体，在45nm工艺下实现1.05pJ/SOP的能效，适合边缘计算应用。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有SNN实现存在两个问题：传统SoC架构存在内存-处理器瓶颈，而大规模神经形态硬件对小规模SNN应用效率低下且浪费。需要专门针对小规模SNN推理优化的解决方案。

Method: 提出SNAP-V神经形态SoC，基于RISC-V架构，包含两种加速器变体：Cerebra-S（总线架构）和Cerebra-H（片上网络架构）。集成RISC-V核心进行管理任务，加速器具有并行处理节点和分布式内存。

Result: 软件和硬件推理结果高度一致，平均精度偏差为2.62%。在45nm CMOS工艺下，平均突触能量为1.05pJ/突触操作（SOP），实现了高能效的SNN推理。

Conclusion: SNAP-V能够实现准确、高能效的SNN推理，适合实时边缘应用，解决了小规模SNN推理的硬件实现问题。

Abstract: Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs) have gained significant attention in edge computing due to their low power consumption and computational efficiency. However, existing implementations either use conventional System on Chip (SoC) architectures that suffer from memory-processor bottlenecks, or large-scale neuromorphic hardware that is inefficient and wasteful for small-scale SNN applications. This work presents SNAP-V, a RISC-V-based neuromorphic SoC with two accelerator variants: Cerebra-S (bus-based) and Cerebra-H (Network-on-Chip (NoC)-based) which are optimized for small-scale SNN inference, integrating a RISC-V core for management tasks, with both accelerators featuring parallel processing nodes and distributed memory. Experimental results show close agreement between software and hardware inference, with an average accuracy deviation of 2.62% across multiple network configurations, and an average synaptic energy of 1.05 pJ per synaptic operation (SOP) in 45 nm CMOS technology. These results show that the proposed solution enables accurate, energy-efficient SNN inference suitable for real-time edge applications.

</details>
