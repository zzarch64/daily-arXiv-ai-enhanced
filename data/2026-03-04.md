### [1] [Pecker: Bug Localization Framework for Sequential Designs via Causal Chain Reconstruction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02583)
*Jiaping Tang,Jianan Mu,Tianyun Ma,Zhiteng Chao,Jing Ye,Huawei Li*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: Pecker是一个针对硬件顺序设计的bug定位框架，通过时间回溯和轨迹剪枝技术重建故障因果链，显著提升了顺序电路中的bug定位效果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 硬件调试耗时费力，其中bug定位占很大比重。现有的基于频谱的bug定位技术在软件领域很成功，但在硬件顺序设计中效果严重下降，因为硬件具有内在的时间特性：bug激活与观察的时间不对齐，以及通过状态元素的渐进错误传播会掩盖根本原因。

Method: 提出Pecker框架，包含两个关键创新：1) 使用估计最小传播周期进行时间回溯，识别潜在的激活周期；2) 策略性轨迹剪枝，消除状态污染效应。

Result: 在组合和顺序电路的全面基准测试中，Pecker在Top-1/3/5排名中分别能定位51%/80%/85%的bug，显著优于现有技术。Pecker在不同电路复杂度下保持稳健性能，而现有方法在顺序设计上表现严重下降。

Conclusion: Pecker通过解决硬件顺序设计中特有的时间对齐和错误传播问题，有效重建故障因果链，为硬件调试提供了更有效的bug定位解决方案。

Abstract: Debugging represents a time-consuming and labor-intensive task in hardware design, with bug localization constituting a substantial portion of this process. While spectrum-based bug localization techniques have achieved remarkable success in software domains and shown promise for hardware description languages, their effectiveness severely degrades in sequential designs. Unlike software programs, hardware designs exhibit intrinsic temporal characteristics that create fundamental challenges: timing misalignment between bug activation and observation, and progressive error propagation through state elements that obscures the root cause. To address these limitations, we propose Pecker, a novel bug localization framework that reconstructs the broken causal chain in sequential designs. Our approach introduces two key innovations: temporal backtracking using Estimated Minimal Propagation Cycles to identify potential activation cycles, strategic trace pruning to eliminate state pollution effects. We evaluate Pecker on comprehensive benchmarks comprising both combinational and sequential circuits. Experimental results demonstrate that Pecker effectively localizes 51%/80%/85% bugs within Top-1/3/5 ranks respectively, significantly outperforming state-of-the-art techniques. Notably, Pecker maintains robust performance across circuit complexities while existing methods exhibit severe degradation on sequential designs.

### [2] [Ouroboros: Wafer-Scale SRAM CIM with Token-Grained Pipelining for Large Language Model Inference](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02737)
*Yiqi Liu,Yudong Pan,Mengdi Wang,Shixin Zhao,Haonan Zhu,Yinhe Han,Lei Zhang,Ying Wang*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: Ouroboros是一种基于晶圆级SRAM的内存计算架构，通过在位执行所有操作消除片外数据传输，显著提升LLM推理的吞吐量和能效。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统LLM推理架构由于频繁的内存层次间数据传输导致高能耗和高延迟，需要新的架构来解决这一问题。

Method: 提出Ouroboros晶圆级SRAM CIM架构，包含三个创新：令牌粒度流水线、分布式动态KV缓存管理、通信感知映射。

Result: 实验结果显示，Ouroboros平均实现4.1倍吞吐量提升和4.2倍能效提升，13B模型峰值达到9.1倍和17倍提升。

Conclusion: Ouroboros通过消除片外数据迁移和优化内存计算，为LLM推理提供了高效节能的硬件解决方案。

Abstract: Conventional LLM inference architectures suffer from high energy and latency due to frequent data movement across memory hierarchies. We propose Ouroboros, a wafer-scale SRAM-based Computing-in-Memory (CIM) architecture that executes all operations in situ, eliminating off-chip migration. To maximize its limited first-level capacity, we introduce three innovations:
  Token-Grained Pipelining: Replaces sequence-level pipelining to mitigate length variations, boosting utilization and reducing activation storage. Distributed Dynamic KV Cache Management: Decouples memory from compute to leverage fragmented SRAM for efficient KV storage. Communication-Aware Mapping: Optimizes core allocation for locality and fault tolerance across the wafer.
  Experimental results show Ouroboros achieves average gains of $4.1\times$ in throughput and $4.2\times$ in energy efficiency, peaking at $9.1\times$ and $17\times$ for the 13B model.

### [3] [Changing the Game: The Bounce-Bind Ising Machine](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02771)
*Haiyang Zhang,Hao Wang,Rui Zhou,Sheng Chang*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: BBIM通过单一可调参数在保持能量景观不变的前提下动态调节自旋动力学，结合Bounce模式（加速逃离局部极小）和Bind模式（快速收敛），在密集MAX-CUT和稀疏3-Regular 3-XORSAT问题上分别实现6.15倍和27.3倍加速，硬件资源消耗可忽略。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Ising模型作为组合优化的基石，其硬件实现面临速度与硬件资源的根本性权衡。现有Ising机器受限于硬件速度瓶颈，需要一种能够在不改变能量景观的前提下动态调节自旋动力学的方法来突破这一限制。

Method: 提出Bounce-Bind Ising Machine (BBIM)，采用单一可调参数调节自旋动力学，基于动态网球/铅球系统类比。Bounce模式加速逃离局部极小值，Bind模式促进快速收敛，两者动态平衡速度与求解质量。

Result: 在密集MAX-CUT问题（边密度=0.5，n=200）上实现峰值6.15倍加速；在稀疏3-Regular 3-XORSAT（二阶）问题（n=160）上实现峰值27.3倍加速。两种情况下硬件资源消耗均可忽略不计。

Conclusion: BBIM为突破Ising机器的硬件速度瓶颈提供了关键路径，在结构不同的基准测试中验证了其在大规模优化硬件中的实际适用性，展示了在不增加硬件资源消耗的情况下显著提升求解速度的能力。

Abstract: The Ising model, originally proposed a century ago, has become a cornerstone of combinatorial optimization in recent decades. However, Ising machines remain constrained by a fundamental hardware-speed trade-off. We introduce the Bounce-Bind Ising Machine (BBIM), a mechanism with a single tunable parameter that modulates spin dynamics without altering the energy landscape, building upon the classic golf-ball analogy but replacing it with a dynamic tennis ball/shot put system. The Bounce mode (accelerating escapes from local minima) and Bind mode (enabling rapid convergence) dynamically balance speed and quality. Benchmarked on dense MAX-CUT (edge density=0.5), BBIM achieves a peak speedup of 6.15 times at n=200. For sparse 3-Regular 3-XORSAT (second-order), the peak speedup reaches 27.3 times at n=160. Both results incur negligible additional hardware resource consumption. This work demonstrates a critical pathway to circumventing the hardware-speed bottleneck and its practical applicability to large-scale optimization hardware, validated on structurally distinct benchmarks.

### [4] [SpecLoop: An Agentic RTL-to-Specification Framework with Formal Verification Feedback Loop](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02895)
*Fu-Chieh Chang,Yu-Hsin Yang,Hung-Ming Huang,Yun-Chia Hsu,Yin-Yu Lin,Ming-Fang Tsai,Chun-Chih Yang,Pei-Yuan Wu*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: SpecLoop：一个基于形式验证反馈循环的RTL到规范生成框架，通过重构RTL并进行等价性验证来确保生成规范的功能一致性


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: RTL实现通常缺乏最新或一致的规范文档，导致理解、维护和验证成本高昂且容易出错。现有基于LLM的RTL到规范生成方法难以确保生成文档准确捕获设计意图。

Method: 提出SpecLoop框架，采用形式验证驱动的迭代反馈循环：1) 生成候选规范；2) 从规范重构RTL；3) 使用形式等价性检查工具验证重构RTL与原始设计的功能一致性；4) 检测到不匹配时，将反例反馈给LLM迭代优化规范，直到证明等价性或无法进一步改进。

Result: 在多个LLM和RTL基准测试上的实验表明，引入形式验证反馈显著提高了规范的正确性和鲁棒性，优于仅使用LLM的基线方法。

Conclusion: 验证引导的规范生成方法有效解决了RTL到规范生成中的意图捕获问题，形式验证反馈循环显著提升了生成规范的质量和可靠性。

Abstract: RTL implementations frequently lack up-to-date or consistent specifications, making comprehension, maintenance, and verification costly and error-prone. While prior work has explored generating specifications from RTL using large language models (LLMs), ensuring that the generated documents faithfully capture design intent remains a major challenge. We present SpecLoop, an agentic framework for RTL-to-specification generation with a formal-verification-driven iterative feedback loop. SpecLoop first generates candidate specifications and then reconstructs RTL from these specifications; it uses formal equivalence checking tools between the reconstructed RTL and the original design to validate functional consistency. When mismatches are detected, counterexamples are fed back to iteratively refine the specifications until equivalence is proven or no further progress can be made. Experiments across multiple LLMs and RTL benchmarks show that incorporating formal verification feedback substantially improves specification correctness and robustness over LLM-only baselines, demonstrating the effectiveness of verification-guided specification generation.
