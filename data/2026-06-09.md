### [1] [Programming Domain-Specific FPGA Hardblocks from HLS: An RTL Blackbox Approach](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.08380)
*Ruthwik Reddy Sunketa,Jeevesh Choudhury,Aman Arora*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出一种编译器无关的方法，通过RTL黑盒抽象（带延迟和间隔契约）使HLS工具可直接从C/C++代码调用FPGA专用硬块（如Tensor Slice），无需手动RTL集成或修改编译器，在保持硬件效率的同时显著提升生产力。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 专用FPGA架构集成了硬块（如Tensor Slice），但编程困难，依赖手动RTL集成，缺乏高层抽象支持。

Method: 将架构硬块封装为带显式延迟和初始间隔契约的RTL黑盒，作为可调度的C级算子暴露给HLS调度器，实现编译器无关的硬块访问和组合。

Result: 在AMD Vitis HLS和VTR工具链上评估，C-Blackbox流程生成的矩阵乘设计在面积-延迟积上优于行为级HLS基线，生产力调整后的效率优于手写RTL。

Conclusion: 领域专用FPGA架构可以通过HLS实现可编程性，同时保持有竞争力的硬件效率。

Abstract: Domain-specific Field Programmable Gate Array (FPGA) architectures increasingly integrate specialized hardblocks, such as Tensor Slices, to accelerate artificial intelligence and machine learning workloads. Despite their efficiency benefits, these architectures remain difficult to program because designers typically rely on manual Register-Transfer Level (RTL) integration to access these hardblocks. This paper presents a compiler-agnostic methodology that enables high-level synthesis (HLS) tools to target custom FPGA hardblocks directly from C/C++ code. Architectural hardblocks are exposed as schedulable C-level operators using an RTL blackbox abstraction with explicit latency and initiation-interval contracts, allowing the HLS scheduler to optimize around specialized hardware without manual RTL orchestration. Unlike traditional uses of HLS blackboxes for external IP integration, our approach treats blackboxes as architectural abstractions, enabling scalable composition of C-level operators that target custom FPGA hardblocks without compiler modification. We evaluate the proposed flow using a Tensor Slice-based FPGA architecture with AMD Vitis HLS and the Verilog-to-Routing (VTR) toolchain. Across multiple matrix sizes, designs generated using the proposed C-Blackbox flow achieve lower area-delay product than behavioral HLS baselines while providing substantially higher productivity-adjusted efficiency than handwritten RTL implementations. These results demonstrate that domain-specific FPGA architectures can be made accessible through HLS while maintaining competitive hardware efficiency.

### [2] [Accuracy-Configurable Floating-Point Multiplier Design for SRAM-Based Compute-in-Memory](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.08430)
*Yiqi Zhou,Junhao Lu,Jiale Yu,Zhuo Xu,Yang He,Yue Yuan,Shan Shen,Daying Sun*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于OpenACM框架的精度可配置浮点乘法器，用于SRAM数字存内计算（DCiM）系统。通过尾数分段近似技术，在保持IEEE 754兼容性的同时，实现了高达69%的逻辑面积缩减和72%的功耗节省，且无延迟开销，在图像处理和ResNet-18推理任务中精度损失可忽略。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有DCiM框架主要支持整数/定点运算，对浮点计算支持有限。直接集成传统IEEE 754浮点单元到SRAM DCiM阵列会导致高昂的面积和功耗开销，迫切需要一种高效、可配置的浮点计算方案。

Method: 首先实现精确的IEEE 754兼容浮点乘法器作为基线，然后提出基于尾数分段（mantissa-segmentation）的近似乘法器，通过分段舍入策略降低硬件复杂度，并将该乘法器集成到OpenACM框架中，支持编译器集成的精度配置。

Result: 后布局结果显示：逻辑面积减少69%，功耗节省72%，且无延迟开销。在图像处理任务和ResNet-18推理上精度退化可忽略不计。

Conclusion: 编译器集成的近似浮点乘法是实现SRAM DCiM系统高效、可配置浮点支持的实用途径，为边缘AI能效计算提供了可行方案。

Abstract: Digital Compute-in-Memory (DCiM) reduces data movement and has become a promising solution for energy-efficient edge AI. However, most existing DCiM frameworks still primarily target integer or fixed-point arithmetic, and provide limited support for compiler-integrated and accuracy-configurable floating-point computation. Directly integrating conventional IEEE 754 floating-point units into dense SRAM-based DCiM arrays, however, incurs high area and power overhead. To address this challenge, this work presents an accuracy-configurable floating-point multiplier integrated into the OpenACM framework for SRAM-based DCiM. An exact IEEE~754-compliant multiplier is first implemented as a baseline, and a mantissa-segmentation-based approximate multiplier is then proposed to reduce hardware cost while preserving numerical fidelity. Post-layout results show up to 69% logic area reduction and 72% power savings over exact floating-point designs without delay overhead. Evaluations on image processing tasks and ResNet-18 inference further demonstrate negligible accuracy degradation. These results indicate that compiler-integrated approximate floating-point multiplication is a practical approach for enabling efficient and configurable floating-point support in SRAM-based DCiM systems. The Floating-Point Multiplier is available on https://github.com/ShenShan123/OpenACM

### [3] [PALUTE: Processing-In-Memory Acceleration via Lookup Table for Edge LLM Inference](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.08891)
*Runyang Tian,Yanru Chen,Weihong Xu,Tajana Šimunić Rosing*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: PALUTE 是一种基于 Monolithic 3D DRAM 的查找表（LUT）内存计算加速器，用于高效边缘 LLM 推理。通过利用 M3D DRAM 的垂直结构实现高并行度 LUT 查询，并配合近内存 LUT 生成器与系统级调度策略，在 Qwen3-4B 模型上实现了 1,264 TPS 吞吐量、0.16W 功耗，能效相比 CHIME 提升 12.8 倍，面积效率相比 PIMPAL 提升 2.0 倍。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 边缘设备上大语言模型推理受限于功耗与面积预算。现有 LUT 方法面临容量和查询延迟开销，亟需一种高效且低面积开销的 LUT 加速方案。

Method: 提出 PALUTE 架构，核心包括：1) 利用 M3D DRAM 垂直结构实现高并行度 in-DRAM LUT 查询；2) 近内存 LUT 生成器支持 GEMM 与逐元素非线性算子；3) 系统级层级调度策略减少跨层数据移动。

Result: 在 Qwen3-4B W4A4 模型上，端到端吞吐量 1,264 TPS，功耗仅 0.16W；能效比 CHIME 提升 12.8×，比 FIGLUT 提升 1.6×；面积效率比 PIMPAL 提升 2.0×。

Conclusion: PALUTE 通过 M3D DRAM 与 LUT 的深度结合，显著提升了边缘 LLM 推理的能效与面积效率，是一种极具前景的边缘推理加速方案。

Abstract: Large language models are increasingly deployed on edge devices with tight power and area budgets. While mixed-precision GEMM reduces arithmetic complexity, quantized inference is often dominated by dequantization and nonlinear operators. Lookup Table (LUT)-based method mitigates these costs by precomputing outputs and replacing repeated arithmetic with table lookups, but existing designs incur significant capacity and lookup-latency overheads. This paper presents PALUTE, a LUT-based Processing-In-Memory accelerator built on Monolithic 3D DRAM for efficient edge LLM inference. PALUTE enables in-DRAM LUT queries that exploit the vertical organization of M3D DRAM memory array tiles to achieve high parallelism with low area overhead. A near-memory LUT generator supports low-latency LUT generation for both GEMM and element-wise unary nonlinear operators, while a system-level tiering and scheduling strategy minimizes data movement across memory tiers. Evaluation using cycle-accurate simulation and RTL synthesis shows that PALUTE achieves 1,264 TPS end-to-end throughput at 0.16 W, improving energy efficiency by 12.8$\times$ over CHIME and 1.6$\times$ over FIGLUT, improving area efficiency by 2.0$\times$ over PIMPAL under W4A4 across Qwen3-4B models.

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 提出了一种基于图相似性的可扩展LLM驱动RTL优化框架，通过三个协作智能体（分区、优化、重构）实现对长上下文、非模块化工业级RTL设计的结构感知优化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有LLM在RTL代码生成与优化中表现优异，但真实世界的RTL设计通常冗长、耦合度高、模块化差，受限于上下文长度限制且缺乏结构化信息，难以直接应用。

Method: 提出三个协作智能体框架：1) 分区智能体：基于AST图相似性将RTL分解为语义有意义的AST子树；2) 优化智能体：利用多模态RAG（AST+RTL指导）生成子模块优化代码；3) 重构智能体：基于逻辑感知排序和Graph-RAG提示重组优化后的子模块，保证全局功能等价性。

Result: 实现了对长上下文RTL设计的鲁棒、结构感知优化，弥合了玩具示例与工业级硬件代码库之间的差距。

Conclusion: 该框架通过图相似性引导的分区与多模态RAG优化，有效解决了长上下文RTL设计的结构优化难题。

Abstract: Large Language Models (LLMs) show great promise in RTL code generation and optimization. However, real-world RTL designs are typically long, entangled, and poorly modularized, posing a major challenge due to context-length limitations and lack of structure. To overcome these obstacles, we propose a scalable LLM-based RTL optimization framework guided by graph similarity. Our method introduces three collaborative agents: (1) a Partition Agent that decomposes RTL designs into semantically meaningful AST subtrees, guided by AST graph similarity to reusable design templates; (2) an Optimization Agent that generates RTL submodule code based on partitioned subtrees using multi-modal Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with both AST and RTL guidance; and (3) a Reconstruction Agent that reassembles optimized submodules based on logic-aware ordering and Graph-RAG prompting, ensuring global functional equivalence. Together, these components enable robust, structure-aware optimization of long-context RTL designs, bridging the gap between toy examples and industrial-scale hardware codebases.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
