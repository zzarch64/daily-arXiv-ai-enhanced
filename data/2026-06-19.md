### [1] [SPINE: A Fault Injection Profiler for Quantized Neural Networks under Accumulated Faults](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19526)
*Nathan Guimarães,Ian Kersz,Leonardo R. Gobatto,Fabio Benevenuti,Michael G. Jordan,Antonio Carlos S. Beck,Fernanda L. Kastensmidt,Jose Rodrigo Azambuja*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于GDB的框架，直接在边缘CPU的二进制上注入累积权重比特翻转，生成逐层故障配置文件，无需模型重训练或代码修改，用于评估量化神经网络在辐射环境下的可靠性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有故障注入研究未能描述在真实内存布局下累积的比特翻转如何转化为预测错误，缺乏对边缘CPU上量化神经网络辐射效应的细粒度分析工具。

Method: 提出GDB驱动的分析框架，直接向边缘CPU的目标二进制注入累积权重比特翻转，生成逐层故障配置文件，无需模型重训练或代码修改。

Result: 在多种拓扑结构、量化方式和内存布局上进行了评估，展示了选择性强化策略应如何应用以有效保护神经网络。

Conclusion: 该方法为量化神经网络在辐射环境下的可靠性评估提供了有效的工具，并指出了选择性硬化策略的方向。

Abstract: Deploying deep neural networks at the edge demands efficient inference under strict cost and power constraints. Quantized neural networks address these demands by replacing floating-point parameters with low-precision integers, yet their weights remain continuously exposed to radiation-induced bit-flips during inference. Fault Injection can be used to simulate those environments, but existing studies fail to characterize how accumulated upsets translate into mispredictions under realistic memory layouts. This paper presents a GDB-driven profiling framework that injects cumulative weight bit-flips directly onto the target binary of edge CPUs, generating per-layer fault profiles without requiring model retraining or code modification. Evaluated across multiple topologies, quantization efforts, and memory layouts, the results indicate how selective hardening strategies should be applied to effectively protect neural networks.

### [2] [A Tool for the Synthesis of Adaptive Probabilistic Processors Based on the Ising Model](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19533)
*Jonathan Juracy Carneiro da Silva,Leonardo R. Gobatto,Jose Rodrigo Azambuja*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 提出了一种自动合成与仿真概率计算架构的工具，可将组合优化问题映射到Ising模型，并自适应选择更新算法，实验表明收敛性和灵活性优于固定方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 组合优化问题求解效率低，现有概率计算方法缺乏自动化的工具支持，且不同算法在不同问题上表现差异大，需要自适应策略。

Method: 自动构建Ising哈密顿量，根据问题特征确定p-bit数量，并在Gibbs Sampling、SA、SQA和基于簇的方法中自适应选择最佳更新算法。

Result: 在基准测试问题上，相比固定方法，收敛行为更优，灵活性更强。

Conclusion: 该框架支持系统性评估概率计算策略，并有助于基于MTJ和p-bit的未来硬件实现。

Abstract: This work presents a tool for the synthesis and simulation of probabilistic architectures for solving combinatorial optimization problems by mapping them to the Ising model. The proposed approach automatically constructs the Ising Hamiltonian and determines the number of probabilistic elements (p-bits) based on problem characteristics such as size and topology. Furthermore, the tool introduces an adaptive strategy for selecting the most suitable update algorithm among Gibbs Sampling, Simulated Annealing (SA), Simulated Quantum Annealing (SQA), and cluster-based methods. Experimental results using benchmark problems demonstrate improved convergence behavior and flexibility compared to fixed approaches. The proposed framework enables systematic evaluation of probabilistic computing strategies and supports the development of future hardware implementations based on MTJs and p-bits.

### [3] [Design and Evaluation of Energy-Efficient Whisper Dot-Product Kernel Offloading on a CGLA Architecture](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19913)
*Takuto Ando,Yu Eto,Ayumu Takeuchi,Yasuhiko Nakashima*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文在可编程粗粒度线性阵列(IMAX)架构上实现并评估了Whisper点积核卸载，通过在ARM Cortex-A72上分析发现点积操作占FP16执行时间的90.6%和Q8_0的87.1%。通过核映射、本地内存大小调整和突发调度，结合FP16-to-FP32内联转换、2路SIMD FMA等优化，在FPGA原型和28nm ASIC投影下，对于Whisper-tiny.en Q8_0实现了11.58J的PDP，比Jetson AGX Orin低2.35倍，比RTX 4090低10.48倍。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Whisper语音模型中的点积核操作是主要性能瓶颈（占执行时间超87%），现有通用架构（GPU/边缘设备）在功耗-延迟积（PDP）上不够高效，需要更优的局部ASR架构方案。

Method: 在IMAX可编程CGLAs架构上实现Whisper点积核卸载，采用核映射、本地内存大小调整、突发调度；使用内联FP16-to-FP32转换、64位数据路径上的2路SIMD FMA、列式多线程、混合执行（对齐向量段在IMAX运行，剩余段在CPU并发执行）。

Result: FPGA原型和28nm ASIC投影（840MHz）下：Whisper-tiny.en的32KB本地内存和突发长度16联合最小化PDP和EDP；TDP交叉对比下IMAX的PDP为11.58J（Q8_0），比Jetson AGX Orin低2.35x，比RTX 4090低10.48x；扩展到Whisper-base.en和small.en时，PDP差距缩小。

Conclusion: IMAX定位为小模型局部ASR场景下可编程的更低PDP架构，特别适合tiny模型规模。

Abstract: In this paper, we implement and evaluate Whisper dot-product kernel offloading on IMAX, a programmable Coarse-Grained Linear Arrays (CGLAs) architecture. Whisper-tiny.en profiling on an ARM Cortex-A72 shows that dot-product operations account for 90.6% of FP16 execution time and 87.1% of Q8_0 execution time. To address this kernel bottleneck, we combine kernel mapping, local-memory sizing, and burst scheduling. The implementation uses inline FP16-to-FP32 conversion, 2-way SIMD FMA on a 64-bit datapath, column-wise multithreading, and mixed execution in which aligned vector segments run on IMAX and residual segments run concurrently on the host CPU. We evaluate the design with an FPGA prototype and a 28nm ASIC projection at 840MHz. For Whisper-tiny.en, 32KB local memory and burst length 16 jointly minimize PDP and EDP. Under a TDP-based cross-platform comparison, the projected IMAX records a PDP of 11.58J for Whisper-tiny.en Q8_0, 2.35x lower than Jetson AGX Orin (27.16J) and 10.48x lower than RTX 4090 (121.38J). The same design extends to Whisper-base.en and Whisper-small.en, where the PDP gap narrows as 32KB local-memory coverage drops from 93.8% for tiny to about 66.5% for base and small. These results position IMAX as a programmable architecture for lower-PDP local ASR in the tiny-model regime.

### [4] [ExSpike: A General Full-Event Neuromorphic Architecture for Exploiting Irregular Sparsity with Event Compression](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20414)
*Yuehai Chen,Farhad Merchant*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: ExSpike是一种通用的全事件驱动神经形态架构，通过数据流优化、专用硬件设计和相邻位置事件压缩技术，在FPGA上实现了高效的脉冲神经网络加速，相比SOTA方案最高提升10倍能效。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有SNN加速器未能充分利用SNN中不规则的时空稀疏性，全事件计算架构尚待探索，难以将稀疏性转化为实际的性能和能效收益。

Method: 1) 提出数据流优化确保每层输入保持脉冲形式，实现纯事件驱动执行；2) 设计ExSpike全事件架构，包含Attention Core用于脉冲驱动的自注意力；3) 引入相邻位置事件压缩，减少空间相邻脉冲序列间的冗余累加。

Result: 在Xilinx Virtex-7 FPGA上实现，分类和分割任务中达到479.15 GOPS、281.85 GOPS/W、0.80 GOPS/W/PE，比SOTA FPGA加速器FireFly-T最高提升10倍PE归一化能效。

Conclusion: ExSpike成功实现了全事件驱动的SNN加速，充分挖掘不规则稀疏性的潜力，在保持高精度的同时显著提升了能效。

Abstract: Spiking neural networks (SNNs) promise energy-efficient computing due to their sparse spatio-temporal activity. However, effectively translating such irregular sparsity into practical performance and energy gains remains challenging, as full-event computing architectures are still underexplored. This paper proposes ExSpike, a general full-event neuromorphic architecture that fully exploits irregular sparsity in SNNs. To realize pure event-driven execution, we first propose a set of dataflow optimizations to ensure that the inputs to each SNN layer remain spike-based, thereby enabling full-event execution throughout the network. We then design a hardware-efficient full-event architecture, named ExSpike, which supports the optimized pure event-driven dataflow and an additional Attention Core for spike-driven self-attention. To further improve computing efficiency, we introduce adjacent-position event compression to reduce redundant accumulations across spatially adjacent spike sequences. ExSpike is implemented on an AMD Xilinx Virtex-7 FPGA and evaluated on both classification and segmentation workloads. Experimental results show that ExSpike achieves high normalized energy efficiency across diverse SNN models while maintaining competitive accuracy, delivering up to 479.15 GOPS, 281.85 GOPS/W, and 0.80 GOPS/W/PE. In particular, ExSpike achieves up to 10$\times$ higher PE-normalized energy efficiency than the SOTA FPGA-based SNN accelerator (FireFly-T). The code for ExSpike is available at \url{https://github.com/xiaoyuehai/ExSpike}.

</details>
