### [1] [SPEAR: A System for Post-Quantization Error-Adaptive Recovery Enabling Efficient Low-Bit LLM Serving](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11244)
*Hongyuan Liu,Yawei Li,Zhiqiang Que,Qinli Yang,Junming Shao,Guosheng Hu*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: SPEAR 提出了一种自适应的后量化误差恢复方法，通过轻量级门控误差补偿器和系统优化，在极低内存开销下缩小4-bit量化模型与FP16的性能差距。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有4-bit量化与FP16之间存在显著质量差距，根本原因在于量化误差是输入依赖且跨token高度变化的，而现有的后量化补偿方法是静态的，对所有输入施加相同校正，导致简单token被过度校正而困难token校正不足。

Method: SPEAR 引入由逐token门控调制的轻量级误差补偿器（EC），通过CKA引导的熵感知诊断将EC仅放置在误差最敏感的层上；同时通过自适应内核融合调度、epilogue集成的对等规约内核、P2P双写以及SLO约束的EC感知调度器解决系统部署挑战。

Result: 在极具挑战性的逐通道量化设置下，SPEAR 恢复了W4与FP16之间困惑度差距的56-75%，额外模型内存开销低于1%，延迟与广泛使用的4-bit服务部署相当。

Conclusion: SPEAR 通过输入自适应的后量化补偿和高效系统设计，显著缩小了低比特量化模型的质量差距，为低成本LLM服务提供了实用方案。

Abstract: Efficient large language model (LLM) serving is increasingly constrained by deployment cost. Quantization is a key technique for reducing serving cost, yet even state-of-the-art 4-bit quantizers exhibit a noticeable quality gap from FP16, particularly for smaller models where low-bit serving is most beneficial. We identify a fundamental cause of this gap: quantization error is highly input-dependent and varies substantially across tokens, while existing post-quantization compensation methods are static and apply identical corrections to all inputs. As a result, easy tokens are over-corrected while hard tokens remain under-corrected.
  We present SPEAR, a system for post-quantization error-adaptive recovery that improves low-bit LLM serving. SPEAR introduces lightweight Error Compensators (ECs) modulated by per-token gates and places them only at the most error-sensitive layers identified through a CKA-guided entropy-aware diagnostic. This focuses a small parameter budget where it is most effective.
  Efficient deployment of ECs presents several systems challenges, including additional computation, tensor-parallel synchronization caused by input-dependent gating, and latency instability across configurations. SPEAR addresses these issues through adaptive kernel-fusion dispatch, combining an epilogue-integrated peer-reduction kernel with P2P dual-write to fuse the post-EC computation into low-bit GEMMs, and an SLO-constrained EC-aware scheduler for predictable serving performance.
  Across challenging per-channel quantization settings, SPEAR recovers 56-75% of the perplexity gap between W4 and FP16 while adding less than 1% model memory overhead and maintaining latency comparable to a widely used 4-bit serving deployment.

### [2] [A Fast Locality Simulator for GEMM Design-Space Exploration on Multi-Chiplet GPUs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11716)
*Euijun Chung,Hyesoon Kim*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文针对多芯粒GPU中的GEMM计算，提出了一种快速的瓦片级局部性模拟器，用于评估不同内核选择（如操作数布局、CTA遍历顺序、数据放置）对跨芯粒远程HBM流量影响的巨大差异（最高达90倍），并利用AI智能体发现2D块交织CTA遍历相比最佳1D遍历可减少高达5.1倍的远程流量。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 多芯粒GPU将内存分为本地和远程HBM区域，减少远程HBM流量对性能和能效至关重要。而GEMM（LLM中的主导算子）的芯粒间流量高度依赖内核设计选择，最优策略并不直观，亟需高效的评估和优化工具。

Method: 开发了一个快速的、功能性的瓦片级局部性模拟器，建模了CTA调度、每芯粒L2缓存以及本地/远程HBM访问，用于评估全尺寸LLM GEMM配置。并以该模拟器作为反馈，使用AI智能体自动探索最优设计。

Result: 在代表性LLM GEMM上，远程流量在设计空间内变化高达90倍；AI智能体发现2D块交织CTA遍历比最佳1D遍历减少最多5.1倍远程流量（轮询数据放置下）。

Conclusion: CTA遍历顺序是影响跨芯粒流量的首要、依赖于GEMM维度的关键设计参数，所提出的模拟器和AI驱动方法能有效指导多芯粒GPU的GEMM优化。

Abstract: Multi-chiplet GPUs split memory into local and remote HBM regions across a silicon interposer, and reducing the remote HBM traffic is crucial for the performance and energy efficiency of multi-chiplet GPUs. For general matrix multiplication (GEMM), the dominant operator in large language models (LLMs), the resulting inter-chiplet traffic depends strongly on kernel choices such as operand layout, CTA traversal order, and data placement, and the optimal strategy to minimize remote accesses is nontrivial. We present a fast, functional, tile-level locality simulator that models CTA scheduling, per-chiplet L2 caches, and local/remote HBM accesses to evaluate a full-size LLM GEMM configuration. Across representative LLM GEMMs, the simulator shows that remote traffic varies by up to 90x across the design space for the same GEMM dimensions. Moreover, using the simulator as feedback, an agentic AI discovers that a 2D block-swizzle CTA traversal reduces remote traffic over the best 1D traversal by up to 5.1x under round-robin placement, identifying CTA traversal order as a first-order, GEMM-dependent design knob for inter-chiplet traffic.

### [3] [Making Locality-aware GEMM Compatible with Page-Granularity Placement on Chiplet GPUs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11718)
*Euijun Chung,Jae Hyung Ju,Hyesoon Kim*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 针对多芯片GPU中非均匀内存访问问题，提出Chiplet-Contiguous Layout（芯片连续布局），通过存储局部数据连续的方式实现局部感知的数据放置，在不修改OS/硬件的前提下，平均减少远程HBM流量24.7倍（Qwen）和19.2倍（Llama）。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 多芯片GPU中内存系统非均匀，芯片间局部性对性能和能效至关重要。传统局部感知调度与固定页面粒度的数据交错不兼容，因为不同GEMM工作负载的最优映射粒度差异很大。

Method: 提出Chiplet-Contiguous Layout，一种全局内存布局，将芯片局部数据连续存储，兼容页面粒度的数据放置，无需修改操作系统或硬件。

Result: 在Qwen 3 30B和Llama 3.1 70B的典型LLM推理和训练GEMM上，相比4KB交错平均减少远程HBM流量24.7倍（Qwen）和19.2倍（Llama）；相比粗粒度局部感知放置分别减少4.1倍和2.1倍。

Conclusion: Chiplet-Contiguous Layout有效解决了多芯片GPU中局部感知数据放置与固定页面粒度交错的不兼容问题，显著降低了远程HBM流量。

Abstract: Multi-chiplet GPUs scale compute throughput and high-bandwidth memory (HBM) capacity, but their non-uniform memory system makes locality between chiplets and their data critical to the GPU's performance and energy efficiency. Locality-aware scheduling and data placement identify which data should reside near each chiplet. However, in general matrix multiplication (GEMM), locality-aware data placement often becomes incompatible with a fixed page-granularity data interleaving, since the optimal granularity for mapping data across chiplets varies widely across workloads. We propose Chiplet-Contiguous Layout, a global memory layout that stores chiplet-local data contiguously. Chiplet-Contiguous Layout enables locality-aware placement compatible with page-granularity placement across diverse large language model (LLM) GEMM shapes, without changes to the operating system or hardware. On representative LLM inference and training GEMMs from Qwen 3 30B and Llama 3.1 70B, Chiplet-Contiguous Layout on average reduces remote HBM traffic by 24.7x on Qwen and 19.2x on Llama over 4KB interleaving, and by 4.1x and 2.1x over coarse locality-aware placement.

</details>


### [4] [BenDi: An Energy-Efficient Quasi-Stochastic Systolic Architecture for Edge Bioelectronics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12235)
*Bochen Ye,Yihan Pan,Shady Agwa,Themis Prodromakis*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出BenDi——一种面向边缘生物电子系统的节能准随机脉动架构，通过电路级到量化级的多层次优化（包括低电压、Bent-Pyramid数据格式、DiP脉动数据流和硬件感知量化），在22nm工艺下相比基线方案实现3.35倍面积缩减和5倍能效提升，仅牺牲1%-3.3%的精度。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 长期连续监测生物医学信号（如ECG）对公共健康至关重要，但AI模型（如CNN）计算资源需求高，传统硬件难以支持资源受限的边缘环境，亟需高能效、小面积的专用架构。

Method: 提出BenDi架构，结合多层优化：低供电电压降低功耗、Bent-Pyramid数据格式实现准随机乘法减少计算开销、DiP脉动数据流优化数据移动、硬件感知量化压缩模型，在保证精度的同时实现高能效边缘推理。

Result: 在22nm工艺、0.5V电压和100MHz频率下，BenDi相比最先进的二值化权重固定脉动架构，面积减小3.35倍，能效提升5倍；相比同类边缘方案，能效和面积效率均提升一个数量级，仅损失1%-3.3%精度。

Conclusion: BenDi在极低硬件预算下实现了高精度CNN边缘推理，验证了多层次协同优化策略在生物电子边缘系统中的有效性，为可穿戴医疗设备等资源受限场景提供了可行的解决方案。

Abstract: Continuous long-term monitoring and diagnosis of biomedical signals, such as electrocardiograms (ECGs), can help mitigate an increasing threat to public health. Artificial Intelligence (AI) models, such as Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), provide accurate monitoring and classification for relevant diseases; however, they require more computational resources than conventional AI hardware can typically afford, especially for a resource-constrained environment on the edge. In this work, we present BenDi, an energy-efficient quasi-stochastic systolic architecture for bioelectronic systems on the edge. BenDi leverages multiple levels of energy and power optimization, ranging from circuits to software quantization, including low supply voltage, the \underline{Ben}t-Pyramid data format for quasi-stochastic multiplication, the \underline{Di}P systolic dataflow, and hardware-aware quantization, to handle CNNs with high accuracy on the edge within limited hardware budgets. The hardware implementation results, using a commercial 22nm technology, show that BenDi architecture, at 0.5 Voltage and 100 MHz, offers 3.35x smaller area and 5x higher energy efficiency, compared to state-of-the-art binary-based weight-stationary systolic architectures. Regarding Bioelectronic edge systems, BenDi achieves an order-of-magnitude improvement in energy efficiency and another order-of-magnitude improvement in area efficiency, compared to its counterparts. This significant improvement comes at the cost of 1\% to 3.3\% accuracy loss on the MIT-BIH and Apnea-ECG benchmarks, respectively, compared with conventional computing using the 32-bit floating-point format.

</details>
