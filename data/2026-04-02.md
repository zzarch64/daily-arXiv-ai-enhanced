### [1] [Sequence-Aware Split Heuristic to Mitigate SM Underutilization in FlashAttention-3 Low-Head-Count Decoding](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.00028)
*Martí Llopart Font,Javier Hernando,Cristina España-Bonet*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 提出序列感知分割策略，解决FlashAttention-3在低头数解码配置中的GPU占用瓶颈，提升解码器内核效率21-24%


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 标准FlashAttention-3启发式方法在低头数解码配置中存在GPU占用瓶颈，因为它仅基于序列长度禁用序列分割，导致Hopper GPU的流多处理器利用率不足

Method: 提出序列感知分割策略，允许在低头数情况下进行序列级并行，提高硬件利用率

Result: 在元数据启用的推理路径上，解码器内核效率提升约21-24%，且未观察到性能回归

Conclusion: 序列感知分割策略有效解决了FlashAttention-3在低头数解码中的硬件利用率问题，显著提升了推理效率

Abstract: The standard FlashAttention-3 heuristic exhibits a GPU occupancy bottleneck in low-head-count decoding configurations because it disables sequence splitting based on sequence length alone, underutilizing the Streaming Multiprocessors of Hopper GPUs. Our proposed sequence-aware split policy mitigates this by allowing sequence-level parallelism in low-head-count regimes, improving hardware utilization to deliver roughly a 21 to 24% improvement in decoder kernel efficiency on metadata-enabled inference paths, with no observed regressions.

### [2] [RePart: Efficient Hypergraph Partitioning with Logic Replication Optimization for Multi-FPGA System](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.00780)
*Zizhuo Fu,Yifan Zhou,Zhaoxin Lu,Guangyu Sun,Runsheng Wang,Meng Li,Yibo Lin*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: RePart：针对多FPGA系统的定制化多层次超图划分框架，通过逻辑复制和拓扑感知优化，显著减少通信距离并提升性能


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有MFS感知划分方法存在两个根本限制：传统超图划分器只关注割边数而忽略拓扑结构；由于保守的平衡裕度导致大量FPGA资源未被利用

Method: 提出RePart框架，在多层次流水线中集成三个协调创新：FPGA感知动态粗化、热值引导分配、复制删除支持的精化

Result: 在Titan23和EDA精英挑战赛基准测试中，RePart平均减少52.3%的总跳数距离，速度提升11.1倍，并优于EDA精英挑战赛获胜者

Conclusion: RePart通过逻辑复制和拓扑感知优化，有效解决了多FPGA系统划分中的通信效率和资源利用问题，为VLSI仿真和快速原型设计提供了高效解决方案

Abstract: Multi-FPGA systems (MFS) are widely adopted for VLSI emulation and rapid prototyping. In an MFS, FPGAs connect only to a limited number of neighbors through bandwidth-constrained links, so inter-FPGA communication cost depends on network topology. This setting exposes two fundamental limitations of existing MFS-aware partitioning methods: conventional hypergraph partitioners focus solely on cut size and ignore topological structure, and they leave substantial FPGA resources unused due to conservative balance margins. We present RePart, a fully customized multilevel hypergraph partitioning framework for MFS that integrates logic replication with topology-aware optimization. RePart introduces three coordinated innovations across the multilevel pipeline: FPGA-aware dynamic coarsening, heat-value guided assignment, and replication-deletion supported refinement. Extensive experiments on the Titan23 and EDA Elite Challenge Contest benchmarks show that RePart reduces total hop distance by 52.3% on average over state-of-the-art hypergraph partitioners with an 11.1x speedup, and outperforms the EDA Elite Challenge winners. Code is available at: https://github.com/Welement-zyf/RePart.

### [3] [Escaping Flatland: A Placement Flow for Enabling 3D FPGAs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01078)
*Cong Hao,Andrew B. Kahng,Bodhisatta Pramanik,Ismael Youssef*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种针对3D FPGA架构的布局流程，通过分区初始化、自适应成本调度、精确延迟估计和模拟退火移动集等改进，相比传统2D布局工具显著降低了关键路径延迟和布线长度。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 3D FPGA通过垂直集成有望获得更高性能，但现有的布局工具主要继承自2D框架，未能捕捉3D架构独特的延迟特性和优化动态，需要专门针对3D FPGA的布局方法。

Method: 提出了一个完整的3D FPGA布局流程，包括：1) 基于分区的初始化；2) 自适应成本调度；3) 精确的延迟估计；4) 针对3D架构的模拟退火移动集。这些改进共同提升了时序估计精度和层分配探索能力。

Result: 相比Verilog-To-Routing (VTR)工具，在四种3D架构上取得了显著改进：3D CB、3D CB-O、3D CB-I和3D SB的关键路径延迟分别降低约3%、2%、3%和6%（最大降低7%、4%、8%和18%）；布线长度分别降低约1%、2%、<1%和5%（最大降低3%、8%、5%和10%）。

Conclusion: 针对3D FPGA架构的专门化布局流程能够有效提升性能，相比传统2D布局方法在时序和布线方面都有显著改进。该工作将在GitHub上开源。

Abstract: 3D field-programmable gate arrays (FPGAs) promise higher performance through vertical integration. However, existing placement tools, largely inherited from 2D frameworks, fail to capture the unique delay characteristics and optimization dynamics of 3D fabrics. We introduce a 3D FPGA placement flow that integrates partitioning-based initialization, adaptive cost scheduling, refined delay estimation, and a simulated annealing move set -- all targeted at 3D FPGA architecture. Together, these enhancements improve timing estimates and the exploration of layer assignments during placement. Compared to Verilog-To-Routing (VTR), our experiments show geometric-mean (max) critical-path delay reductions of ~3% (~7%), ~2% (~4%), ~3% (~8%), and ~6% (~18%) for four 3D architectures: 3D CB, 3D CB-O, 3D CB-I, and 3D SB, respectively. We also achieve geometric-mean (max) routed wirelength reductions of ~1% (~3%), ~2% (~8%), < 1% (~5%), and ~5% (~10%), respectively. Our work will be permissively open-sourced on GitHub.

</details>
