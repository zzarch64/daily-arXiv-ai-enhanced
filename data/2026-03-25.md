*Jingtian Dang,Ritik Raj,Changhai Man,Jianming Tong,Tushar Krishna*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: SCALE-Sim TPU：针对TPU风格加速器的验证和扩展版SCALE-Sim v3，通过硬件验证、轻量级学习延迟模型和现代ML框架集成，提升周期精确模拟的准确性、覆盖范围和实用性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有周期精确模拟器在验证真实硬件和集成现代ML编译器栈方面存在不足，限制了其在脉动阵列加速器研究中的准确性和可用性。

Method: 1. 使用Google TPU v4测量数据验证SCALE-Sim的脉动GEMM模型；2. 为非脉动逐元素操作引入轻量级学习延迟模型；3. 集成基于StableHLO的前端，支持JAX和PyTorch等现代ML框架直接模拟。

Result: 模拟周期数与硬件延迟呈现强线性相关，可实现简单周期到延迟映射；非脉动操作的延迟模型相对误差中位数低于3%；通过统一编译器IR实现端到端延迟估计显著改进。

Conclusion: SCALE-Sim TPU通过硬件验证、学习延迟模型和现代编译器集成，提升了周期精确模拟在TPU上全模型性能分析的保真度、覆盖范围和实用性。

Abstract: Cycle-accurate simulators are widely used to study systolic accelerators, yet their accuracy and usability are often limited by weak validation against real hardware and poor integration with modern ML compiler stacks. This paper presents SCALE-Sim TPU, a validated and extended version of SCALE-Sim v3 for TPU-style accelerators. Specifically, we make three contributions: (1) We validate SCALE-Sim's systolic GEMM model against measurements on Google TPU v4 and show that simulated cycle counts exhibit a strong linear correlation with hardware latency, enabling a simple cycle-to-latency mapping. (2) We introduce lightweight learned latency models for non-systolic elementwise operations, achieving median relative errors below 3 percent using only tensor size and shape, substantially improving end-to-end latency estimation. (3) We integrate a StableHLO-based frontend that allows workloads from modern ML frameworks such as JAX and PyTorch to be simulated directly via a unified compiler IR. Together, these contributions improve the fidelity, coverage, and practicality of cycle-accurate simulation for whole-model performance analysis on TPUs.

### [2] [CPU Simulation with Ranked Set Sampling and Repeated Subsampling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22598)
*Magnus Ekman*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 该论文提出使用排名集抽样(RSS)和重复子抽样方法来改进计算机架构模拟中的代表性区域选择，相比传统随机抽样能显著缩小置信区间并降低误差。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 计算机系统模拟研究通常只能执行有限数量的短应用程序区域，因为完整的端到端模拟耗时过长。现有方法使用随机抽样或基于阶段的表征来识别代表性区域，但这些方法在置信区间和准确性方面存在改进空间。

Method: 1) 在计算机架构模拟中重新审视随机抽样，分析样本标准差与样本均值的关系；2) 应用统计文献中成熟的排名集抽样(RSS)技术，该技术自然映射到架构模拟；3) 引入重复子抽样方案来识别代表性模拟区域，并评估两种选择标准。

Result: RSS相比简单随机抽样能将置信区间宽度减少高达50%。对于固定样本量，重复子抽样方法将最大观测误差从35%降低到10%。在SPEC CPU 2017整数应用程序中，模拟30个各100万指令的区域时，平均误差低于2%，最大误差为3.5%。

Conclusion: 排名集抽样和重复子抽样方法能显著提高计算机架构模拟的准确性和效率，为未来的模拟研究提供更可靠的代表性区域选择方案。

Abstract: Computer system simulation studies routinely rely on executing a limited number of short application regions, since full end-to-end simulation is prohibitively time-consuming. To preserve representativeness, existing methods employ either random sampling or phase-based characterization to identify representative regions.
  In this work, we revisit random sampling in the context of computer architecture simulation. To assess how the confidence level varies with different micro-architectural configurations, we examine how the sample standard deviation relates to the sample mean. We show that the ranked set sampling (RSS) technique - well established in the statistical literature - maps naturally to architectural simulation and yields significantly tighter confidence intervals than simple random sampling. Across our experiments, RSS reduces the confidence interval width by up to 50%.
  We further introduce a repeated subsampling scheme that identifies representative simulation regions for future studies. For a fixed sample size, this approach reduces the maximum observed error from 35% to 10%. Evaluating two selection criteria, we find that more informed subsample selection provides additional accuracy gains. Overall, our method achieves an average error below 2% and a maximum error of 3.5% across individual SPEC CPU 2017 Integer applications when simulating 30 regions of 1 million instructions each.

### [3] [CPU Simulation Using Two-Phase Stratified Sampling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22605)
*Magnus Ekman*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文重新审视了SimPoint方法在CPU性能模拟中的局限性，提出了一种两阶段分层采样框架，将最大应用误差从40-60%降低到3%，同时将所需样本量减少一个数量级。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 计算机架构研究中，完整端到端应用模拟耗时过长。行业标准解决方案SimPoint通过选择少量代表性代码区域来捕获程序阶段行为，但作者发现常用SimPoint数量可能导致显著的估计误差。

Method: 1) 将SimPoint重新构建为分层采样问题，推导保守置信区间；2) 将SimPoint分解为分层（聚类）和样本单元选择（质心选择）两个基本组件；3) 扩展为两阶段（双重）采样方案，使用大量初步随机样本来改进分层和区域选择。

Result: 使用20个SimPoint时，两个应用表现出40-60%的性能预测误差。采用提出的两阶段分层框架后，最大每应用误差降至3%，同时所需样本量相比简单随机采样减少一个数量级。

Conclusion: 提出的两阶段分层框架为架构模拟提供了统计基础和高效路径，显著提高了性能预测准确性，同时大幅减少了模拟时间。

Abstract: Simulation remains a cornerstone of computer architecture research, yet full end-to-end application execution is prohibitively time-consuming. The industry-standard solution, SimPoint, mitigates this cost by selecting a small number of representative code regions that capture program phase behavior. In this work, we take a fresh look at phase behavior in the SPEC CPU 2017 Integer suite to assess how pronounced such behavior truly is and what accuracy can be expected from typical SimPoint usage. Based on previously published data, we argue that common SimPoint counts can induce substantial estimation errors. To explore this further, we recast SimPoint as a stratified sampling problem, which enables the derivation of a conservative confidence interval. The analysis indicates that significant errors are expected, and our empirical analysis confirms this: with 20 SimPoints, two applications exhibit 40-60% performance prediction error.
  We decompose SimPoint into its two fundamental components - stratification (clustering) and sample-unit selection (centroid choice) - and analyze their individual effects on accuracy. We then extend the approach into a two-phase (double) sampling scheme, in which a large preliminary random sample enables improved stratification and more representative region selection. Using this method, the maximum per-application error drops to 3%. Finally, we demonstrate that the proposed two-phase stratified framework achieves an order-of-magnitude reduction in required sample size compared to simple random sampling while maintaining a tight analytical confidence interval, suggesting a practical path toward statistically grounded and efficient architectural simulation.

### [4] [Characterizing CPU-Induced Slowdowns in Multi-GPU LLM Inference](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22774)

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 多GPU系统在LLM推理和服务中常因CPU资源不足导致性能下降，增加CPU核心数能显著提升性能而不需额外GPU


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究发现大规模机器学习工作负载在多GPU系统中的性能瓶颈往往不是GPU饱和，而是CPU无法为GPU提供足够的工作负载，导致GPU利用率低下。这个问题在采用进程级分离和CUDA Graphs等现代优化的服务栈中依然存在。

Method: 通过对现代大型语言模型推理和服务工作负载的详细研究，系统分析了CPU引起的多GPU LLM推理瓶颈。评估了在有限CPU分配下系统的表现症状，并比较了增加CPU核心数对性能的影响。

Result: 在中等服务负载下，CPU资源不足的配置经常超时，而提供足够CPU资源可以恢复响应性，将首次令牌延迟（TTFT）降低1.36-5.40倍，显著提高GPU利用率，且无需额外GPU。

Conclusion: CPU配置是多GPU LLM推理配置中的关键因素，增加CPU核心数相对于GPU实例定价的边际成本很小，却能显著提升性能和稳定性，有助于防止控制侧瓶颈。

Through a detailed study of modern large language model (LLM) inference and serving workloads, we find that multi-GPU performance frequently degrades not because GPUs are saturated, but because CPUs fail to keep the GPUs busy. Under limited CPU allocations, systems exhibit symptoms such as delayed kernel launch, stalled communication, and increased tokenization latency, leading to severe GPU underutilization even when ample GPU resources are available. This work presents a systematic analysis of CPU-induced slowdowns in multi-GPU LLM inference. We show that these bottlenecks persist even in serving stacks that employ process-level separation and modern GPU-side optimizations such as CUDA Graphs. Since the marginal cost of additional CPU cores is small relative to GPU instance pricing, our evaluation indicates that increasing the number of CPU cores can substantially improve performance and stability at minimal additional cost. Under moderate serving load, we observe that CPU-starved configurations frequently time out, while providing adequate CPU resources restores responsiveness and reduces time-to-first-token (TTFT) latency by 1.36-5.40x across configurations, all without requiring additional GPUs. This work shows that CPU provisioning is a crucial factor in multi-GPU LLM inference configuration, helping prevent control-side bottlenecks.

### [5] [TorR: Towards Brain-Inspired Task-Oriented Reasoning via Cache-Oriented Algorithm-Architecture Co-design](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22855)

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: TorR是一个面向边缘部署的算法-架构协同设计，用超维计算关联推理器替代CLIP密集对齐，通过查询缓存、δ更新和门控旁路实现实时低功耗目标检测


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 算法层面：将对齐重新表述为超维相似度和图组合，引入部分相似度重用（查询缓存、δ更新、相似度/负载门控旁路）。架构层面：实现车道可扩展的位切片项内存，带有银行/精度门控和轻量级控制器，调度旁路/δ/完整路径以满足实时目标

The design exposes deployment-time configurability (effective dimension $D'$, thresholds, precision) to trade accuracy, latency, and energy for edge budgets.

### [6] [TRINE: A Token-Aware, Runtime-Adaptive FPGA Inference Engine for Multimodal AI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22867)
*Hyunwoo Oh,Hanning Chen,Sanggeon Yun,Yang Ni,Suyeon Jang,Behnam Khaleghi,Fei Wen,Mohsen Imani*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: TRINE是一个单比特流FPGA加速器和编译器，用于统一执行多模态推理，无需重新配置，通过模式可切换引擎、流内token剪枝和依赖感知层卸载，在嵌入式平台上实现低延迟和高能效。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 多模态模型（混合ViT、CNN、GNN和Transformer NLP）在嵌入式平台上运行困难，因为它们的计算/内存模式不同，且硬实时目标几乎没有余量。现有解决方案需要重新配置或无法有效处理多样化的计算模式。

Method: 1. 将各层统一为DDMM/SDDMM/SpMM操作；2. 设计模式可切换引擎，在运行时切换权重/输出固定脉动阵列、1xCS SIMD和可路由加法树；3. 宽度匹配的两阶段top-k单元实现流内token剪枝；4. 依赖感知层卸载（DALO）在可重构处理单元间重叠独立内核执行。

Conclusion: TRINE通过单一比特流实现了统一的多模态推理加速，在嵌入式平台上提供了最先进的延迟和能效，适用于视觉、语言和图计算工作负载。

Abstract: Multimodal stacks that mix ViTs, CNNs, GNNs, and transformer NLP strain embedded platforms because their compute/memory patterns diverge and hard real-time targets leave little slack. Layers are unified as DDMM/SDDMM/SpMM and mapped to a mode-switchable engine that toggles at runtime among weight/output-stationary systolic, 1xCS SIMD, and a routable adder tree (RADT) on a shared PE array. With int8 quantization, accuracy drops remain <2.5% across representative tasks, delivering state-of-the-art latency and energy efficiency for unified vision, language, and graph workloads-in one bitstream.

</details>
