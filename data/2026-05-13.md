### [1] [Sieve: Dynamic Expert-Aware PIM Acceleration for Evolving Mixture-of-Experts Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11277)
*Jungwoo Kim,Rubens Lacouture,Genghan Zhang,Gina Sohn,Qizheng Zhang,Swapnil Gandhi,Christos Kozyrakis,Kunle Olukotun*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文针对现代MoE模型推理中专家分布高度不均衡（双峰分布）导致PIM系统效率低下的问题，提出了Sieve运行时框架，通过在GPU和HBM-PIM之间动态调度专家执行，并重叠计算与通信，在多个模型上实现了1.3-1.6倍的吞吐量和交互性提升。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有PIM系统基于静态规则卸载内存密集型操作，未考虑MoE推理中负载不均衡和GPU间通信的联合影响。现代MoE模型激活专家数少但专家总数多，导致双峰专家分布（少量专家处理大量token，大量专家处理极少token），不同专家的算术强度差异巨大，严重降低了现有PIM系统的效率。

Method: 提出Sieve运行时框架，包含一个调度器，根据运行时token-to-expert分布，在GPU和HBM-PIM之间划分专家执行，同时考虑互联开销、内存带宽、GPU和PIM吞吐量。Sieve通过重叠GPU计算、PIM计算以及设备内/设备间通信来协调多GPU及附带的HBM-PIM栈的执行。

Result: 在基于Ramulator 2.0的周期精确模拟器上评估，相比现有最先进的MoE PIM系统，Sieve在Qwen3.5-397B-A17B、GPT-OSS-120B和Qwen3-30B-A3B上分别实现了1.3倍、1.3倍和1.6倍的吞吐量和交互性提升。

Conclusion: 通过动态感知token分布并联合调度GPU和PIM计算，可以有效解决MoE推理中因专家分布双峰化导致的PIM效率下降问题，实现更优的吞吐量和交互性。

Abstract: Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) has become a dominant architecture for scaling large language models (LLMs). However, the execution characteristics of MoE inference are changing rapidly and increasingly mismatch the assumptions underlying existing Processing-in-Memory (PIM) systems. Prior PIM systems for LLMs rely on static rules to offload memory-bound operations to PIM, without accounting for the combined effects of load imbalance and inter-GPU communication. Meanwhile, modern MoE models activate fewer experts out of increasingly many, creating a bimodal expert distribution: a small set of experts receives many tokens, while a long tail of experts receives only one or a few.
  We identify a trend in modern MoE models toward increasingly bimodal token-to-expert distributions, quantify the resulting disparity in arithmetic intensity across experts, and show that this disparity dramatically reduces the efficiency of state-of-the-art PIM systems for LLMs. To address this problem, we propose a scheduler for serving MoE models on multi-GPU systems with attached HBM-PIM stacks. Our scheduler partitions expert execution between GPU and PIM based on runtime token-to-expert distributions, while jointly considering interconnect overhead, memory bandwidth, GPU throughput, and PIM throughput. Moreover, we propose Sieve, a runtime framework that employs the scheduler to coordinate execution across GPUs and their attached HBM-PIM stacks. Sieve overlaps GPU computation, PIM computation, and intra- and inter-device communication while preserving cross-device dependencies induced by expert parallelism. Sieve is evaluated on our cycle-accurate simulator based on Ramulator 2.0. Compared to state-of-the-art PIM systems for MoE, Sieve improves both throughput and interactivity by 1.3x, 1.3x, and 1.6x on Qwen3.5-397B-A17B, GPT-OSS-120B, and Qwen3-30B-A3B, respectively.

### [2] [Heterogeneous SoC Integrating an Open-Source Recurrent SNN Accelerator for Neuromorphic Edge Computing on FPGA](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12217)

Motivation: SNN在边缘设备上具有低功耗和并行计算优势，但数字神经形态硬件的流片成本高昂，阻碍了其广泛推广。FPGA作为灵活且低成本的替代平台，可以促进开源硬件设计的发展。

Abstract: The growing popularity of Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs) and their applications has led to a significant fast-paced increase of neuromorphic architectures capable of mimicking the spike-based data processing typical of biological neurons. The efficient power consumption and parallel computing capabilities of the SNNs lead researchers towards the development of digital accelerators, which exploit such features to bring fast and low-power computation on edge devices. The spread of digital neuromorphic hardware however is slowed down by the prohibitive costs that the silicon tape out of circuits brings, that's why targeting Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs) could represent a viable alternative, offering a flexible and cost-effective platform for implementing digital neuromorphic systems and helping the spread of open-source hardware designs. In this work we present an heterogeneous System-on-Chip (SoC) where the operations of ReckOn, a Recurrent SNN accelerator, are managed through the integration with traditional processors. These include the RISC-V-based, open-source microcontroller X-HEEP and the ARM processor featured in Zynq Ultrascale systems. We validate our design by reproducing the classification results through the implementation on FPGA of the taped-out version of ReckOn in order to check the equivalence of the accuracy and the characteristics in terms of physical implementation. In a second set of experiments, we evaluate the online learning capability of the solution in classifying a subset of the Braille digit dataset recently used to compare neuromorphic frameworks and platforms.

### [3] [Enhancing Instruction Prefetching via Cache and TLB Management](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12433)
*Alexandre Valentin Jamet,Georgios Vavouliotis,Marti Torrents,Dimitrios Chasapis,Marc Casas*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出IP-CaT框架，通过翻译预取缓冲区(tPB)和三分支指令预取替换策略(TIPRP)两项技术，协同优化TLB和缓存管理以提升L1I预取效果。在105个服务器负载下，IP-CaT+EPI相比单独EPI实现8.7%的几何平均加速。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有L1I预取器存在两个关键局限：跨页预取需要地址翻译导致延迟，以及预取指令行的复用行为高度异质（有些被多次复用，有些则"死胎"）。这些问题限制了L1I预取器的性能潜力。

Method: IP-CaT包含两个组件：(1) 翻译预取缓冲区(tPB)，与二级TLB协同放置的小型结构，存储跨页L1I预取获取的页表项，降低翻译开销；(2) 三分支指令预取替换策略(TIPRP)，基于决策树的L2缓存替换策略，专门优化L1I预取获取的缓存行。

Result: 在105个现代服务器负载下，IP-CaT与三种先进L1I预取器(EPI、FNL+MMA、Barca)结合均有一致性能提升。IP-CaT+EPI相比单独EPI实现8.7%几何平均加速。IP-CaT优于现有指令TLB预取、先进TLB替换策略(CHiRP)以及多种缓存替换策略(Emissary、SHiP++、Mockingjay)。

Conclusion: 首次提出联合优化TLB和缓存管理的L1I预取微架构框架IP-CaT，通过解决跨页翻译延迟和预取行复用异质性两大问题，显著提升服务器工作负载性能。

Abstract: Modern server workloads exhibit massive instruction footprints that heavily pressure the processor front-end, making L1 instruction (L1I) prefetching critical for sustaining performance. However, this paper shows that current L1I prefetchers fail to reach their full potential due to two key limitations. First, L1I prefetches crossing page boundaries require address translation before issuance, and translation latency reduces prefetch timeliness. Second, the reuse behavior of code lines fetched by L1I prefetches is highly heterogeneous: while some lines are reused many times, others are dead-on-arrival.
  This paper introduces Instruction Prefetch-Centric Cache and TLB Management (IP-CaT), the first microarchitectural framework jointly optimizing TLB and cache management for L1I prefetching. IP-CaT consists of two components: (i) the translation Prefetch Buffer (tPB), a small structure colocated with the second-level TLB (sTLB) that stores page table entries fetched by page-crossing L1I prefetches, reducing translation overheads; and (ii) the Trimodal Instruction Prefetch Replacement Policy (TIPRP), a decision-tree-based L2 cache replacement policy specialized for lines fetched by L1I prefetches.
  We evaluate IP-CaT with three state-of-the-art L1I prefetchers: EPI, FNL+MMA, and Barca. Across 105 contemporary server workloads, IP-CaT consistently improves performance. For example, IP-CaT+EPI achieves an 8.7% geomean speedup over EPI alone. We further show that IP-CaT outperforms state-of-the-art instruction TLB prefetching, advanced TLB replacement (CHiRP), and state-of-the-art code-aware, prefetch-aware, and general-purpose cache replacement policies, including Emissary, SHiP++, and Mockingjay.
