### [1] [BitFair: A 12nm Bit-Serial CNN Accelerator with Learnable Early Termination and Adaptive Bit Ordering for Ultra-Low-Power XR Vision](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05445)
*Ang Li,Chang Gao*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: BitFair是一种面向扩展现实（XR）可穿戴设备的软硬件协同设计的比特串行CNN加速器，通过可学习的比特级提前终止和自适应比特排序，在亚毫秒延迟下实现高能效（117.0 BTOPS/W，0.07 pJ/SOP），相较此前方案能效提升4.0-22.1倍，准确率最高提升9.2%。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: XR可穿戴设备需要在极低功耗（几瓦）和严格延迟预算（运动到光子延迟<20ms，神经网络推理仅几毫秒）下实现始终在线的感知计算。现有的比特串行架构即使ReLU输出为零仍会处理所有比特位，造成不必要的能耗浪费。

Method: 提出软硬件协同设计的BitFair方法：(1) 可学习的逐层阈值，在部分累积和可靠预测最终ReLU输出为零时触发比特级提前终止；(2) 自适应比特排序，优先处理信息量更高的比特位，最大化提前终止机会而不损失准确率。在GlobalFoundries 12nm FinFET工艺上实现。

Result: 核心面积0.34 mm²，104 KB片上存储器，电压范围0.55-0.70V。在IBM DVS128 Gesture和N-MNIST数据集上分别达到96.5%和97.7%准确率。实现亚毫秒延迟、117.0 BTOPS/W能效和0.07 pJ/SOP。相比此前XR视觉加速器，能效提升4.0-22.1倍，准确率提升最高9.2%。

Conclusion: BitFair通过动态比特级稀疏性利用和自适应比特排序，成功在XR场景的严苛功耗与延迟约束下实现了高能效高精度CNN推理，验证了软硬件协同设计在极端资源受限场景下的有效性。

Abstract: Extended Reality (XR) wearables require always-on perception within tight power envelopes of a few watts and motion-to-photon latency budgets below 20 ms, leaving only a few milliseconds for neural-network inference. Bit-serial computing is attractive for such energy-efficient neural network acceleration, but many existing architectures still process all bits even when ReLU sets the final output to zero. This paper presents BitFair, a software-hardware co-designed bit-serial CNN accelerator with learnable bit-level early termination and adaptive bit ordering, working under the ultra-low-power and strict latency requirements of XR applications. BitFair exploits dynamic bit-level sparsity by learning per-layer thresholds that trigger early termination when partial sums reliably predict that the final ReLU output will be zero. Furthermore, it searches for layer-wise bit orders that prioritize informative bits, maximizing early termination without sacrificing accuracy. A GlobalFoundries 12nm FinFET implementation with a core area of 0.34 mm^2, 104 KB on-chip memory, and voltage scaling from 0.55 to 0.70 V achieves sub-millisecond latency, up to 117.0 BTOPS/W, and 0.07 pJ/SOP. On IBM DVS128 Gesture and N-MNIST, BitFair achieves 96.5% and 97.7% accuracy, respectively, while improving effective energy efficiency by 4.0-22.1x and accuracy by up to 9.2% over prior fabricated XR vision accelerators.

Method: 采用CMOS技术实现神经形态硅神经元（SiLIF-DBS控制器）；构建简化的计算模型并嵌入帕金森皮层-基底节框架；以STN-LFP的β频段平均整流值（Beta ARV）作为控制生物标志物进行闭环验证。

Abstract: Parkinson's disease (PD) affects millions worldwide and causes severe motor symptoms. Adaptive deep brain stimulation (aDBS) delivers physiologically informed stimulation that can track fluctuations in PD motor symptoms, enabling more intelligent DBS control. However, most existing aDBS approaches are primarily algorithm- and software-driven, with limited efforts toward circuit realization, particularly low-power and implantable integrated circuits. This paper presents the Silicon Leaky Integrate-and-Fire Deep Brain Stimulation (SiLIF-DBS) controller, a neuromorphic silicon neuron stimulator implemented with metal-oxide-semiconductor (CMOS) technology. For system-level evaluation, a simplified computational model of the SiLIF-DBS controller is derived and embedded within a Parkinsonian cortico-basal ganglia framework for closed-loop validation. Our SiLIF-DBS controller for aDBS suppresses pathological beta activity while consuming only 25% of the power required by open-loop stimulation and achieving a suppression efficiency of $5.85\%$/$μ$W. Overall, our SiLIF-DBS controller achieves strong beta suppression at substantially reduced power, delivering high suppression efficiency that demonstrates it is a viable foundation for low-power implantable aDBS.

### [3] [Is Your NPU Ready for LLMs? Dissecting the Hidden Efficiency Bottlenecks in Mobile LLM Inference](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05475)
*Guanyu Cai,Ruiming Tian,Lang Yang,Zhouhong Ren,Jinliang Yuan,Lingkun Li,Jiliang Wang*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文首次对移动端大语言模型推理进行跨层测量研究，覆盖5种框架和3种硬件后端，发现NPU上框架差异可达10倍、计算密集与内存密集阶段的异质性、以及后端特定调度导致的40%能耗浪费，并提出可降低54.8%能耗的最优配置。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 移动端部署LLM受硬件效率瓶颈严重制约，但缺乏对各类框架和后端跨层、细粒度的能耗分析工具与系统性理解。

Method: 开发了PowerBench细粒度分析工具，支持后端特定能耗归因；对5种主流框架和CPU/GPU/NPU三种后端进行跨层测量实验。

Result: NPU上框架差异可达10倍；NPU擅长计算密集的预填充阶段而CPU擅长内存密集的解码阶段；调度不当（线程配置、NPU休眠延迟、CPU轮询间隔）导致40%能耗浪费。

Conclusion: 基于发现提出了面向能耗的移动LLM推理最佳实践配置，预计可在NPU后端上降低最多54.8%的能耗。

Abstract: Deploying Large Language Models (LLMs) on mobile devices enhances privacy and reduces latency, but is severely bottlenecked by hardware inefficiency. We present the first comprehensive, cross-layer measurement study of mobile LLM inference, uniquely spanning five mainstream frameworks (e.g., llama.cpp, GENIE) and three hardware backends (CPU, GPU, NPU). To enable this analysis, we develop PowerBench, a fine-grained profiling tool that provides the first backend-specific energy attribution, moving beyond traditional device-level measurements. Our study yields three critical insights: (1) Framework-induced performance gaps are substantially amplified on NPUs, reaching up to 10x using custom operators due to divergent offloading and quantization strategies. (2) We identify a distinct phase split where NPUs excel at compute-bound prefilling, while CPUs outperform all other backends in memory-bound decoding. This is driven by the NPU's preference for large, fixed-shape workloads, which conflicts with the small-kernel, dynamic nature of decoding. (3) Backend-specific profiling uncovers substantial scheduling headroom missed by prior work. Suboptimal thread configurations, uncoordinated NPU sleep latencies, and CPU polling intervals result in up to 40% energy waste. Leveraging these findings, we present an energy-oriented best-practice configuration for mobile LLM inference. We estimate that this configuration could reduce energy consumption by up to 54.8% on the NPU backend across three datasets.

### [4] [NEMESIS: NEtlist-Driven Modeling and Equation Synthesis with Inversion-Aware SPICE Anchoring](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05657)
*Subhadip Ghosh,Ramesh Harjani,Sachin S. Sapatnekar*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 提出NEMESIS框架，利用大语言模型结合SPICE验证来生成跨导运算放大器（OTA）的精确性能方程，在保持<7%误差的同时实现~4622倍加速。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: OTA设计中存在快速近似模型与精确但昂贵的SPICE仿真之间的权衡，亟需一种既能保证精度又能大幅提升效率的设计方法。

Method: NEMESIS首先识别电路基本单元，利用先前相似OTA的方程或LLM从电路输入推导初始方程，再通过SPICE修复循环迭代优化，平衡解析模型速度与SPICE精度。

Abstract: This work presents NEMESIS, a multimodal framework for operational transconductance amplifier (OTA) design using large language models (LLMs). The framework begins with equations retrieved from the prior invocations of NEMESIS to structurally similar OTAs, if available; otherwise, it uses the LLM to derive the initial equations directly from the circuit input. These equations are iteratively refined via a SPICE-based repair loop. In a commercial 65nm PDK, NEMESIS is demonstrated on five OTA topologies, producing SPICE-verified equations across biasing ranges with <7% average relative error and a post-convergence evaluation speedup of ~4622x over full SPICE-based evaluation.

### [5] [Boosting FPGA Performance with Direct BRAM-DSP Paths](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05756)
*Jiajun Hu,Ruthwik Reddy Sunketa,Andrew Boutros,Aman Arora*

Main category: cs.AR

TL;DR: 本文提出一种轻量级FPGA架构增强方案，通过在BRAM和DSP之间添加专用直连通路，避免数据经过全局路由网络，配合改进的布局算法，在Agilex-10类FPGA上实现DL层设计最高+25% Fmax提升和-49%线长缩减，且对非DL基准无影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: FPGA上DL工作负载中，BRAM与DSP之间的数据传输必须经过全局路由网络，导致线长增加、布线拥塞和关键路径延迟，成为性能瓶颈。

Method: 1) 在BRAM和DSP块之间引入专用直连连接，绕过全局互连；2) 改进布局算法，使其能够识别并优化这些BRAM-DSP宏块。

Result: 在Agilex-10类FPGA上，针对常见DL层设计，最高实现+25% Fmax提升和-49%线长减少，且面积和延迟开销可忽略不计。

Conclusion: 该方案以极小的硬件开销显著提升DL工作负载性能，同时保持与非DL基准的兼容性和QoR稳定性，具备良好的商业可行性。

Abstract: Efficient data movement between memory and compute units is a key performance bottleneck in modern FPGA designs, particularly for deep learning (DL) workloads. In typical FPGA architectures, data transfers between block RAMs (BRAMs) and digital signal processing units (DSPs) must traverse the global routing network, leading to increased wirelength, routing congestion, and critical-path delays. Prior work has explored in- and near-BRAM compute architectures to mitigate these issues, but such solutions often require fundamental changes to FPGA architecture and CAD tools, limiting their commercial viability. This paper proposes a lightweight architectural enhancement that introduces a dedicated direct connection between BRAM and DSP blocks, enabling BRAM data to be consumed by DSPs without passing through the global interconnect. We also enhance the placement algorithm to recognize these BRAM-DSP macro blocks. The proposed architectural change incurs negligible area and delay overhead and does not affect non-DL benchmarks, while the proposed CAD remains compatible with the baseline architecture, where it yields negligible change in quality-of-results (QoR). On an Agilex-10-like FPGA, the proposed architecture and CAD updates deliver up to +25% Fmax and -49% wirelength on common DL layer designs.
